Writing • Level 2

Elementary Writing

Expand your writing skills. Practice narrating past events, describing future plans, writing comparisons, and fixing common elementary errors.

💡 10 Writing Topics

Choose a topic below and write a short paragraph (5-7 sentences) about it.

1. Yesterday: Write about what you did yesterday from morning to night.
2. Next Weekend: Use 'going to' to explain your plans for next Saturday.
3. Daily Habits: Describe things you 'always' do and 'never' do.
4. Compare Cities: Compare your hometown to another city using -er and more.
5. A Bad Trip: Write a short story about a time you traveled and something went wrong.
6. Best Movie: Give your opinion on a movie and use 'because' to explain why.
7. Food Habits: What do you usually eat for breakfast vs dinner?
8. A Promise: Use 'will' to write 3 promises to your best friend.
9. Directions: Write instructions on how to get from your school to your house.
10. My Outfit: Describe what you are wearing today and how much it costs.
