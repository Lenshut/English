Speaking Tasks

Practice contrasting objects and people verbally using comparatives.

Task A: Read Aloud

"My brother is taller than me, but I am faster than him."

Task B: Read Aloud

"A train is faster than a car, but a plane is faster than a train."

Task C: Read Aloud

"Today is hotter than yesterday, but tomorrow will be colder."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"Let's compare animals! Is a cat bigger than a mouse?"
You:
"Yes, a cat is bigger than a mouse."
Teacher:
"Is a dog faster than a cheetah?"
You:
"No! A cheetah is faster than a dog."

Product Review: Laptop A vs Laptop B

Today we are looking at two computers: Laptop A and Laptop B. Laptop A is $500, but Laptop B is $800. This means Laptop A is cheaper than Laptop B.

However, Laptop B is faster than Laptop A. If you want to play games, Laptop B is better.

Laptop A is very small and light. Laptop B is bigger than Laptop A. It is also heavier. Laptop A is older than Laptop B. Laptop A came out in 2020, and Laptop B came out in 2025. Which one do you want?
