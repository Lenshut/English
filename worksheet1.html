🗣️ Speaking Tasks

Practice these aloud with a partner or teacher.

01

Say your name and age loudly in a full sentence.

02

Spell your first name out loud, letter by letter.

03

Point to three objects in the room and state their colors (e.g., "The bag is blue").

04

Command your partner to "stand up" and "sit down".

05

Point to a male classmate and say a full sentence starting with "He".

06

Make a sad face and say "I am sad."

07

Ask the teacher a location question using "Where".

08

Point to an object and ask your partner "What is this?"

09

Mime and say three morning routine verbs (e.g., wake up, eat, drink).

10

Tell the class what your mother or father does at 8 o'clock (remember the 's'!).
