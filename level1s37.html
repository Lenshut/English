Speaking Tasks

Read the texts aloud clearly. Focus on asking the price question smoothly. Then, roleplay.

Task A: Read Aloud

"Excuse me. How much is this apple? It is two dollars."

Task B: Read Aloud

"How much is that big book? It is ten dollars."

Task C: Read Aloud

"I want to buy this pen. How much is it? It is one dollar. Thank you."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (In a store)
Teacher:
"Hello! Welcome to the store. Do you want to buy an apple?"
You:
"Yes, please. How much is this apple?"
Teacher:
"It is two dollars."
You:
"Okay, I want it. Here is two dollars. Thank you."
7

Reading

Read the story about shopping at the big store and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Anna. Today, I go to the store with my mother. The store is very big. We want to buy food and clothes. I see a big, red apple. I like apples. I ask the tall man in the store, "Excuse me, how much is this apple?" The man smiles and says, "It is two dollars." My mother says, "I want the apple, please." We buy the red apple.

Then, we walk to the clothes. I see a blue shirt. I like the blue shirt very much. I say to my mother, "Look at this shirt! I want it." My mother looks at the shirt. She asks the nice woman, "Excuse me, how much is this shirt?" The nice woman says, "It is twenty dollars." My mother says, "Thank you. We want the blue shirt, please."

Next, we see a big book. I like to read books at home. I ask the man, "How much is this book?" The man says, "It is ten dollars." I say, "Thank you." We do not buy the book today. We buy the apple and the blue shirt.

We go to the man to pay the money. The apple is two dollars. The shirt is twenty dollars. My mother gives the man the money. She says, "Thank you very much." The man says, "You're welcome!" I am very happy. I have a good apple and a nice shirt. It is a good day at the big store.

Questions:

1. How much is the apple?
2. What color is the shirt?
3. How much is the shirt?
4. How much is the book?
