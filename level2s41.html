7

Reading

Read the text message thread negotiating a hangout plan and time.

Tom: Hi Anna! Are you free on Saturday?
Anna: Hi Tom! No, I am sorry. I am busy on Saturday. I have to work.
Tom: Oh, okay. What about Sunday? Are you free on Sunday?
Anna: Yes, I am free on Sunday!
Tom: Great! Do you want to go to the new Italian restaurant with me?
Anna: I would love to, but I do not like Italian food. Let's eat Mexican food instead!
Tom: That sounds perfect. Let's meet at 7 PM.

Questions:

1. Is Anna free on Saturday?
2. Why is she busy on Saturday?
3. Is she free on Sunday?
4. Does she want to eat Italian food?
5. What do they decide to eat instead?
