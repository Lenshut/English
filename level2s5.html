Short Answer

Answer the questions by combining the three ideas into ONE fluid sentence using at least two different conjunctions.

1. Name two foods you like, and one you do not.

2. Why do you study English, and what is the result?

6

Speaking Tasks

Practice telling a multi-step story using multiple conjunctions aloud.

Task A: Read Aloud

"I like to read and I like to watch movies, but I do not like to play sports because they are tiring."

Task B: Read Aloud

"We went to the beach because it was sunny, and we played games, so we had a lot of fun."

Task C: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"Tell me about your typical Sunday. Give me lots of details!"
You:
"On Sunday, I wake up late and I eat a big breakfast, but I do not go to work because it is the weekend."
Teacher:
"That sounds great. What do you do in the afternoon?"
You:
"In the afternoon, I am usually tired, so I relax at home and I read a book."
7

Reading

Read the complex narrative below and break it down to answer the questions.

Yesterday was a very long day. I woke up at six o'clock and I made coffee, but there was no milk, so I drank it black. I was tired because I did not sleep well the night before.

I walked to the bus stop, but the bus was late, so I had to take a taxi because I didn't want to miss my meeting. At work, my boss was happy because I finished the project, and he gave me a new task.

After work, I wanted to go to the gym and play basketball, but I was too hungry, so I went to a restaurant instead. I ate pizza and salad, but I didn't eat dessert because I was full.

Questions:

1. Why did the person drink black coffee?
2. Why was the person tired?
3. Why did they take a taxi?
4. Why was the boss happy?
5. Why did they not eat dessert?
