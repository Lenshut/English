Reading

Read the argumentative essay below and extract the specific supporting reasons.

Why I Prefer the Country to the City

Many people love living in a big city, but I prefer the country to the city. First, I prefer the country because it is very quiet. The city is too loud with cars and people. Second, I prefer the country because the air is clean and healthy. In the city, the air is often dirty.

My friend David disagrees. He prefers the city because it is exciting. He likes the city because there are many restaurants and cinemas.

However, I prefer cooking at home to going to restaurants because it is cheaper. Ultimately, I think the country is the best place for a relaxing life.

Questions:

1. Why does the author prefer the country (first reason)?
2. Why does the author prefer the country (second reason)?
3. Why does David prefer the city?
4. Why does David like the city (specific places)?
5. Why does the author prefer cooking at home to going to restaurants?
