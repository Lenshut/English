"Hello, Teacher. Are you a student?"

"No, I am not. I am a teacher. Are you a student?"

"Yes, I am."
Teacher:
"(Points to a classmate) Is he a teacher?"

Boy: Yes, I am. My name is Leo.

Teacher: Hello, Leo. Is she your friend?

Boy: Yes, she is. Her name is Emma.

Teacher: Hello, Emma. Are you a student?

Girl: Yes, I am.

Teacher: Are you ten years old?

Girl: No, I am not. I am nine years old.

Teacher: Is Leo nine years old?

Girl: No, he is not. He is ten years old.

Teacher: Very good. Welcome!
