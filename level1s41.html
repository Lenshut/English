Speaking Tasks

Read the texts aloud clearly. Emphasize the prepositions "next to" and "across from". Then, roleplay.

Task A: Read Aloud

"The store is next to the school. They are very close."

Task B: Read Aloud

"The hospital is across from the park. There is a street between them."

Task C: Read Aloud

"Where is my house? It is next to the big tree and across from the store."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"Excuse me. Where is the big hospital?"
You:
"The hospital is next to the school."
Teacher:
"Is it across from the park?"
You:
"Yes, it is across from the park. Turn right here."

Reading

Read the story describing Anna's town and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Anna. Welcome to my town. It is a very big town. I want to tell you about the places in my town. My house is on a long street. It is a nice house. Next to my house, there is a big park. The park has tall trees and green grass. I like to play in the park with my friends. We run and we are happy.

Across from the park, there is a big school. The school is very big. I go to school in the morning. I study English and I read books. Next to the school, there is a small store. I go to the store to buy apples and water. The store is next to the school.

Where is the hospital? The hospital is far. It is not near my house. The hospital is across from a big tree. My father works at the hospital. He is a doctor. He goes to the hospital in his car.

In my town, we have many good places. The park is next to my house. The school is across from the park. The store is next to the school. The hospital is far. I like my town very much. Where is your house? Is it next to a park or across from a school? Do you have a big store in your town?
