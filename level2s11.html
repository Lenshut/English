Short Answer

Write complex sentences detailing your habits by answering the prompts below.

1. What do you usually do in the morning, and why?

2. Name something you always do, but your friend never does.

3. Where do you sometimes go on the weekend, and who with?

4. What do you never eat, and why?

5. Name something you usually do after work/school, and what is the result? (use 'so')

6. Describe a busy day (use 'always' and 'and').

7. Describe a lazy day (use 'never' and 'because').

8. What do you usually drink, but what do you sometimes drink?

9. Why are you usually tired in the evening?

10. Write one long sentence about your perfect Sunday. (use 'usually', 'and', 'because').

Speaking Tasks

Practice presenting a complex daily routine aloud.

Task A: Read Aloud

"I usually wake up at 7:00 AM, and I always drink coffee, but I never eat breakfast because I am not hungry."

Task B: Read Aloud

"He sometimes plays football on Saturday, so he is usually very tired on Sunday."

Task C: Read Aloud

"We always go to the supermarket on Friday, but we never buy fast food because it is bad for us."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"Tell me about your morning routine."
You:
"I usually wake up early and I always drink tea, but I never eat breakfast."
Teacher:
"Why do you never eat breakfast?"
You:
"I never eat breakfast because I am usually late for work!"
7

Reading

Read the detailed daily routine and answer deep temporal questions.

My name is Sarah and I have a very busy daily routine. I always wake up at 6:00 AM because I work far from my house. I usually take the bus to work, but sometimes I take a taxi if I am late. I never drive because I do not have a car.

At work, I am always busy and I usually drink three cups of coffee. I like my job, but it is very difficult, so I am usually tired in the evening.

After work, I usually go to the gym, but sometimes I just go home and relax. I always go to bed at 10:00 PM because I need to sleep well.

Questions:

1. Why does Sarah always wake up at 6:00 AM?
2. When does she sometimes take a taxi?
3. Why does she never drive?
4. Why is she usually tired in the evening?
5. What does she usually do after work, and what does she sometimes do?
