Proficiency Exercises

Test your knowledge of Proficiency concepts: Supreme Moderation, Cognitive Biases, Steelmanning, Macro-Synthesis, and Capstone Thesis Formulation.

✍️ Fill in the Blanks

1. To ______ your argument, you believe that taxes stifle growth. (steelman/strawman)
2. Let's ______ these disparate views into a single holistic approach. (synthesize/analyze)
3. This initiative aims to overhaul operations, ______ reducing overall waste. (thereby/therefore)
4. The central thesis of my ______ posits that innovation requires risk. (inquiry/question)
5. If I could just ______, I'd like to redirect this conversation. (interject/interrupt)
6. That decision is heavily influenced by confirmation ______. (bias/fallacy)
7. They are operating in an ______ chamber of their own design. (echo/sound)
8. I'd like you to ______ this project going forward. (spearhead/do)
9. We must weigh these concerns against the cost of ______. (inaction/action)
10. It is a ______ that being hyper-connected makes us isolated. (paradox/irony)
