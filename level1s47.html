Reading

Read the story synthesizing all Level 1 concepts and answer the final questions.

Hello! My name is Ben. I am a tall boy and I am a student. I am happy today. I wake up at 7 o'clock in the morning. I drink cold water and I eat an apple. I like apples. I do not like hot tea. My father is a tall man. He wakes up at 6 o'clock. He goes to work.

At 8 o'clock, I go to school. The school is a big building. It is next to a park and across from a store. I study English in the classroom. The teacher is a nice woman. I say, "Excuse me, where is the book?" She says, "It is on the desk." I say, "Thank you!"

After school, I go to the store. I want to buy a blue shirt. I ask the man, "How much is the blue shirt?" The man says, "It is ten dollars." I buy the shirt.

In the evening, I go home. My house is very big. I play with my dog. The dog is fast. It runs under the table. We eat dinner at 7 o'clock. We eat meat and rice. At 9 o'clock, I am tired. I sleep in my bed. It is a great day.

Questions:

1. What time does Ben wake up?
2. What does Ben drink in the morning?
3. Where is the school? (next to...)
4. How much is the blue shirt?
5. Where is the dog?
