Speaking • Level 2

Elementary Speaking

Move beyond single sentences. Practice answering past/future questions, comparing items, describing your habits, and performing practical real-world roleplays.

⏳ Time Travel Q&A

Answer the teacher's questions out loud using the correct Past (Did/Was) or Future (Will/Going to) tense.

1. What did you eat for dinner yesterday?
2. Are you going to study English this weekend?
3. Where did you go last Saturday?
4. What will you do if it rains tomorrow?
5. Did you watch a movie last night?
6. Where are you going to travel next year?

⚖️ Compare Pictures

Look at the emojis. Make comparative (-er/more) or superlative (-est/most) sentences aloud.

🐢 vs 🐇 Prompt: Compare speed (Fast/Slow)
🚲 vs 🚗 vs ✈️ Prompt: Who is the fastest?
📱 vs 💻 Prompt: Compare price (Expensive)
🍔 vs 🥦 Prompt: Compare health (Good/Bad)

🔄 Habit Description

Speak for 30 seconds about your habits using Always, Usually, Sometimes, and Never.

Prompt: Describe your typical Sunday.

  • What time do you usually wake up?
  • What do you always eat?
  • What is something you sometimes do in the afternoon?
  • What is something you never do on Sunday?

📖 Short Story Narration

Connect the 3 ideas using 'and', 'but', or 'because' to tell a fluid story aloud.

Story 1:

I went to the store. / I wanted to buy apples. / The store was closed.

Story 2:

Yesterday it rained. / I did not go to the park. / I stayed home.

Story 3:

I am studying English. / I want to travel. / English is difficult.

🎭 Practical Roleplay

Act out the scenario with your partner. Keep the conversation going for 3 turns.

Scenario A: The Restaurant

You are a customer. Ask the waiter if they have fish. Order food, but ask for it without onions. Ask for the price.

Scenario B: Lost in Town

You are lost. Stop a stranger (partner). Ask where the hospital is. Follow their directions (Left, Right, Straight) and thank them.
