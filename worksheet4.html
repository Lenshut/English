Intermediate Exercises

Test your knowledge of Intermediate concepts: Relative Clauses, Advanced Connectors, Embedded Questions, Conditionals, and Business Phrasal Verbs.

✍️ Fill in the Blanks

1. The man ______ lives next door is a doctor.
2. I have ______ studying English for three years.
3. ______ it was raining, we went to the park.
4. The job pays well. ______, the hours are flexible. (Also/In addition)
5. Do you know where the station ______?
6. In my ______, working from home is more productive.
7. I like sports. To be ______, I like tennis.
8. While it is true that cars are fast, I ______ trains are safer.
9. I am not ______ sure, but I suppose we could try that.
10. It ______ to be very cold in January, so bring a coat.
