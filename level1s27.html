"I read a book. You read a book. We read a book."

Task B: Read Aloud

"They go to school. They write in the classroom."

Task C: Read Aloud

"We play in the park. We run and we drink water. They play too."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"What do you and your friends do at school?"
You:
"We study English. We read and we write."
Teacher:
"What do they do after school?"
You:
"They go to the park. They play football."
7

Reading

Read the story and answer the questions below.

Hello! I am Mark and you are my friend. We are students. We go to school in the morning. We study English and we read books. We like our school. It is a big school with many tall trees.

Look at the boys and girls. They are students too. They walk to school. They talk and they laugh. They do not work. They study. In the classroom, we sit at our desks. We write in our books. We listen to the teacher. You read a big book and I read a small book. We are happy.

After school, we go to the park. We play football with our friends. They run fast and they kick the ball. We play and we drink cold water. We do not drink hot tea in the park.

In the evening, I go to my house and you go to your house. We eat dinner with our family. My parents are at home. They work in the morning, but they rest at night. They eat hot food. After dinner, we sleep. I sleep in my room and you sleep in your room. We wake up the next day and we do it again. We like our routine.

Questions:

1. What do Mark and his friend do in the morning?
2. What do they read in the classroom?
3. Do the boys and girls work?
4. Where do they go after school?
5. When do Mark's parents work?
