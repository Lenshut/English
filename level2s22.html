Speaking Tasks

Practice inquiring about plans and cancelling plans aloud.

Task A: Read Aloud

"Are you going to study tonight? No, I am not going to study."

Task B: Read Aloud

"Is he going to play football? Yes, he is going to play at 5 PM."

Task C: Read Aloud

"I am not going to go to the party because I am too tired."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (The Fortune Teller)
Psychic:
"I see your future! Are you going to travel to London next year?"
You:
"No, I am not going to travel to London. I don't have money."
Psychic:
"Ah, okay. Are you going to buy a new house?"
You:
"Yes, I am! I am going to buy a very big house."

Reading

Read the cancellation email below and identify what plans are not going to happen.

To: Mark
Subject: Change of Plans for the Weekend

Hi Mark,

I am sorry, but I am not going to visit you this weekend. My car is broken, and I am not going to take the bus because it is too slow.

We are not going to go to the museum on Saturday. Also, I am not going to eat at the famous restaurant with you on Sunday. I need to save my money to fix my car.

Are you going to be free next weekend? Maybe I can visit then. Let me know!

Best,
David

Questions:

1. Is David going to visit Mark this weekend?
2. Is David going to take the bus?
3. Are they going to go to the museum?
4. Is David going to eat at the restaurant?
5. What question does David ask Mark at the end?
