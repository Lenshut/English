Writing • Level 8

Proficiency Writing

Demonstrate absolute mastery. Formulate capstone theses, diagnose cognitive biases, unravel paradoxes, and synthesize ethical frameworks into elite academic prose.

💡 10 Writing Topics

Choose a topic below and write a master-level synthesis paragraph (15-20 sentences) demonstrating absolute rhetorical dominance.

1. The Master Thesis: Formulate a thesis using ('The central thesis of my inquiry posits...').
2. Diagnosing Irrationality: Critique a business error using cognitive biases ('confirmation bias', 'sunk cost').
3. The Perfect Steelman: Summarize an opponent's view flawlessly before destroying it.
4. Embracing the Paradox: Analyze a societal contradiction ('It is a paradox that...').
5. Ethical Architecture: Defend a controversial policy using a 'Utilitarian perspective'.
6. Absolute Concession: Defeat an argument while granting its premise ('Even if we assume...').
7. The Executive Pivot: Write a memo reeling in a chaotic team ('Let's pivot back to the core objective').
8. Synthesizing Chaos: Summarize 3 disparate views into a 'general consensus... albeit'.
9. Soft Power Delegation: Assign heavy tasks diplomatically ('I'd like you to spearhead...').
10. Visionary Rhetoric: Pitch a product that 'fundamentally redefines' humanity.

Read the flawed reasoning in each scenario and diagnose the cognitive bias or logical fallacy at play using C2 vocabulary.
