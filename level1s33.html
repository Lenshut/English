7

Reading

Read the story about friends in the park and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Sarah. I am with my friends today. We are in the big park. It is a very hot day, and we are very thirsty. I talk to my friend, Mark. "Mark, do you like water?" I ask. Mark says, "Yes, I do. I like cold water on a hot day." I give Mark cold water. He drinks the water.

Then I ask my friend, Lily. "Lily, do you like milk?" Lily smiles and says, "No, I do not. I do not like milk. Do you have juice?" I say, "Yes, I do! I have sweet apple juice. Do you like apple juice?" Lily says, "Yes, I do! Thank you."

My teacher is in the park too. I go to my teacher. "Hello, teacher! Do you like hot tea?" The teacher says, "No, I do not. It is a hot day today. I do not like hot tea today." I ask, "Do you like cold water?" The teacher says, "Yes, I do. Cold water is very good."

We sit under a big tree. We drink our drinks and we are happy. I ask my friends about food. "Do you like apples?" I ask. Mark says, "Yes, I do." Lily says, "No, I do not. I like bananas." We talk and we play. It is fun to ask questions. Do you like the park? Do you like cold water on a hot day? What do you like to drink?
