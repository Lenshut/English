Speaking Tasks

Read the restaurant lines aloud clearly. Then, roleplay.

Task A: Read Aloud (Customer)

"Excuse me! I want hot meat and rice, please."

Task B: Read Aloud (Waiter)

"Here is the meat and rice. Do you want water?"

Task C: Read Aloud (Customer)

"Yes, cold water, please. I do not like hot tea. Thank you very much!"

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (Restaurant)
Teacher:
"Hello! Welcome. What do you want to drink?"
You:
"I want cold water, please. I am hot."
Teacher:
"Okay. Do you like fish?"
You:
"No, I do not like fish. I want meat, please. Thank you."
7

Reading

Read the story about Leo's family at a restaurant and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Leo. Today is a very good day. My family and I go to a restaurant. We are very hungry. We sit at a big table. The restaurant is very big and very nice. A tall man comes to the table. He is the waiter. He says, "Hello! Excuse me, what do you want to drink?"

My father says, "I want cold water, please." My mother says, "I want hot tea, please. Thank you." I do not want water and I do not want tea. I say, "Excuse me. I want cold apple juice, please." The waiter says, "You are welcome!"

Then, we want to eat food. The waiter asks, "Do you want fish or do you want meat?" My father likes meat. He says, "I want hot meat and white rice, please." My mother does not like meat. She likes fish. She says, "I want fish and bread, please."

I do not like fish and I do not like meat today. I am a young boy. I say, "I want a big apple and a yellow banana, please. I like sweet fruit." The waiter smiles and gets the food.

We eat the good food and we drink our drinks. The food is very good. My father eats his meat. My mother eats her fish. I eat my fruit. After dinner, my father says, "Thank you very much!" The waiter says, "You're welcome. Have a good night!" We are a very happy family today.

Questions:

1. What does the mother want to drink?
2. What does Leo want to drink?
3. Does the mother like meat?
4. What does the father want to eat?
5. What does the father say after dinner?
