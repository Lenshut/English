Speaking Tasks

Read the plural sentences out loud to your teacher.

"We are students. They are teachers."

Task B: Read Aloud

"We are boys and they are girls."

Task C: Point and Say

Point to yourself and a friend: "We are..." Point to others: "They are..."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"Look at you and me. What are we?"
You:
"We are friends."
Teacher:
"(Points to two other students) What are they?"
You:
"They are students."
