✅ True or False

"Many companies are adopting a four-day work week. On one hand, employees report higher satisfaction and better mental health. Furthermore, productivity rarely drops. On the contrary, some studies show it actually increases. However, implementing this system is difficult for customer service departments, which must remain open 24/7."
1. Employees are generally happier with a four-day work week.
2. Productivity usually goes down when people work fewer days.
3. A four-day work week is easy for all departments to implement.
4. The author believes productivity actually increases in some cases.
5. Customer service departments must be available every day.
