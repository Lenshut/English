Speaking Tasks

Read the texts aloud smoothly. Emphasize "do not". Then, roleplay.

Task A: Read Aloud

"I like apples, but I do not like bananas. I do not like fish."

Task B: Read Aloud

"We do not eat cheese. They do not drink hot water."

Task C: Read Aloud

"The dog likes meat. It does not like bread. We do not eat bad food."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"Hello! Do you like fish?"
You:
"No, I do not like fish. I do not eat it."
Teacher:
"What food do you not like?"
You:
"I do not like cheese and I do not like bread. I like meat."
7

Reading

Read the story about Sam and his family's food dislikes, then answer the questions below.

Hello. My name is Sam. I am a student. I want to tell you about food. Food is very good, but I do not like all food. I like apples and bananas. I like sweet fruit. But, I do not like fish. Fish is not good. I do not eat fish for dinner.

My sister is a young girl. Her name is Lily. Lily likes fish. She eats fish in the evening. But, Lily does not like bread. She does not eat bread in the morning. She eats white rice.

My father and my mother like meat. They eat hot meat and white rice. They drink cold water. My father does not like cheese. He says cheese is not good. My mother likes cheese. She eats cheese with bread.

We have a big dog. The dog likes meat. The dog eats a lot of meat. But, the dog does not like fruit. The dog does not eat apples or bananas.

At dinner, we sit at the big table. I do not eat fish, my father does not eat cheese, and the dog does not eat fruit. We eat the food we like. We talk and we are happy. What food do you not like? Do you like fish or do you not like fish?

Questions:

1. What food does Sam not like?
2. What food does Lily not like?
3. What food does Sam's father not like?
4. Does the dog eat apples?
5. Do they eat the food they do not like?
