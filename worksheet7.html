Mastery Exercises

Test your knowledge of Mastery concepts: Meta-discourse, Strategic Repetition, Legal/Executive Register, PR Spin, and Philosophical Defense.

✍️ Fill in the Blanks

1. The ______ of the new system reduced errors significantly. (implement/implementation)
2. What needs to be ______ is the long-term impact. (prioritize/prioritized)
3. I didn't want to go, but he ______. (did/do)
4. ______ to this argument is the assumption of risk. (Crucial/Crucially)
5. It is ______ upon us to fix this issue immediately. (incumbent/imperative)
6. We must unequivocally ______ the actions of those involved. (condemn/praise)
7. The crux of the ______ is our ethical obligation. (matter/issue)
8. Detractors will ______ argue that this is too expensive. (inevitably/maybe)
9. I am willing to ______ that the rollout was flawed. (concede/deny)
10. Failure to comply will render the contract ______ and void. (null/none)
