Speaking Tasks

Perform the final Level 1 Graduation Roleplay with your teacher. Speak clearly and confidently.

Task: Graduation Interview
Teacher:
"Hello. Welcome. Please tell me about yourself."
You:
"Hello. My name is [Name]. I am [Age] years old. I am a student."
Teacher:
"Very good. Tell me about your morning routine."
You:
"I wake up at [Time]. I eat and I drink water. I go to school."
Teacher:
"Excellent. Now, ask me a question."
You:
"Excuse me, teacher. Do you like English?"
Teacher:
"Yes, I do! Thank you for a great class."
You:
"You're welcome. Goodbye!"
7

Reading

Read the final comprehensive story and answer the questions. You are ready for Level 2!

Hello! My name is Leo. I am ten years old and I am a student. This is my English book. It is a big, blue book. I am very happy today. I want to tell you about my family and my town.

My family is very nice. My father is a tall man. He wakes up at six o'clock in the morning. He goes to work at the big hospital. The hospital is across from the park. He does not like hot tea. He likes cold water. My mother is a short woman. She works at a store. The store is next to the bank. She likes sweet fruit.

I go to school at eight o'clock. I study English and I read books. I have a pen in my bag. At twelve o'clock, I eat lunch. I do not like fish. I eat meat and rice.

In the afternoon, I want to play. I go to the park. The park has tall trees. I play with my dog. The dog is very fast. I am hot, so I ask my mother, "Excuse me, I want water, please." My mother says, "Here is the water." I say, "Thank you!"

At night, we eat dinner. Then, I am very tired. I sleep in my bed at nine o'clock. I like my family, I like my town, and I like English!

Questions:

1. How old is Leo and what does he do?
2. Where does Leo's father work?
3. What does Leo eat for lunch?
4. Is Leo polite when he is hot?
5. What time does Leo sleep?
