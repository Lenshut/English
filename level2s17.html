Speaking Tasks

Practice an interrogation-style interview aloud with a partner.

Task A: Read Aloud (Interrogation)

"Did you go to the bank yesterday? Did you see the money?"

Task B: Read Aloud (Answers)

"Yes, I did go to the bank. No, I didn't see the money."

Task C: Read Aloud

"Did she call you last night? Did she talk about the test?"

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (The Detective)
Detective:
"Where were you last night? Did you stay at home?"
You:
"Yes, I did. I stayed at home all night."
Detective:
"Did you watch TV? Did you hear anything?"
You:
"I watched TV, but I didn't hear anything strange."
7

Reading

Read the interview transcript and answer the past question comprehension.

Interviewer: Hello, Mr. Smith. I have some questions about your weekend. Did you stay in the city?
Mr. Smith: No, I didn't. I went to the mountains with my family.
Interviewer: That sounds nice. Did you have good weather?
Mr. Smith: Yes, we did. It was sunny and very warm.
Interviewer: Did you go hiking?
Mr. Smith: Yes, we did. We walked for three hours. We were very tired.
Interviewer: Did you eat at a restaurant?
Mr. Smith: No, we didn't. We cooked our own food. We made a big dinner on Saturday night.
Interviewer: Did you enjoy your weekend?
Mr. Smith: Yes, I did! It was fantastic.

Questions (Answer Yes, he did / No, he didn't / etc.):

1. Did Mr. Smith stay in the city?
2. Did they have bad weather?
3. Did they go hiking?
