Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"I have a big problem. I am very hungry."
You:
"You are hungry, so you should eat a sandwich."
Teacher:
"But I do not have any money today!"
You:
"You do not have money, so I will buy it for you."
7

Reading

Read the passage below and extract the cause-and-effect relationships using 'so'.

Today is Saturday, so David does not have to go to the office. He has a lot of free time today, so he decides to have a very active and exciting day. He wakes up late at nine o'clock, so he is very rested and full of energy. The weather outside is warm and sunny, so he puts on a light t-shirt and his running shoes. He wants to exercise, so he goes to the large park near his house with his dog, Max. Max is a very energetic dog, so he runs very fast across the grass. They play together for two hours, so they both get very tired and sweaty.

After the park, David is extremely thirsty, so he stops at a small shop and buys a large bottle of cold water. He drinks it very quickly, so he feels much better. Later in the afternoon, David wants to see his friends, so he calls them on the phone. They are all free, so they agree to meet in the city center at two o'clock. They are all very hungry after a long morning, so they decide to go to a famous Italian restaurant. The restaurant is very popular, so they have to wait for thirty minutes to get a table. The food is incredibly delicious, so they eat a lot of pizza and pasta. David eats too much, so his stomach feels very full and heavy.

In the evening, the sky becomes dark and cloudy, so the weather gets quite cold. Suddenly, it starts to rain very heavily, so David and his friends cannot walk home. They do not have umbrellas, so they quickly take a taxi to stay dry. David arrives home at eight o'clock. He had a fantastic day with his friends and his dog, so he goes to bed feeling very happy and relaxed.

Questions:

1. Today is Saturday, so what does David not have to do?
2. The weather is sunny, so where does David go?
3. David is extremely thirsty, so what does he buy?
4. The friends are very hungry, so where do they go?
5. It starts to rain heavily, so how do they go home?
