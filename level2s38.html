Reading

Read the 'getting to know you' dialogue and spot the reciprocating questions.

At the Party

John: Hi, my name is John. I am a teacher. What about you?
Sarah: Nice to meet you, John. I am Sarah. I work in a bank.
John: That's interesting! Do you live near here?
Sarah: Yes, I live in the city center. And you?
John: I live outside the city. It is very quiet.
Sarah: I prefer the city because there is a lot to do. I like to visit museums on the weekend. How about you?
John: I like to go to the park and read books.

Questions:

1. How does John return the question after saying his job?
2. What is Sarah's job?
3. How does Sarah return the question about where she lives?
4. What does Sarah like to do on the weekend?
5. How does Sarah ask John about his weekend?
