7

Reading

Read the habit survey below and extract the data based on the 'How often' responses.

Interviewer: Hello, welcome to our lifestyle podcast. Today, we are talking to Tom about his daily habits. Tom, thank you for joining us. Let's start with technology. How often do you use your smartphone?
Tom: I always use my smartphone. I use it every single hour! I need it for work, to talk to my friends, and to read the news.

Interviewer: Wow, that is a lot of screen time. How often do you read a physical book?
Tom: I never read books. I know it is a bad habit, but I prefer to watch videos or listen to podcasts when I want to learn something new. Books make me sleepy.

Interviewer: I see. Moving on to health, how often do you exercise or play sports?
Tom: I sometimes play football with my friends on Saturday afternoons, but not always. If the weather is bad, we stay inside. I want to exercise more, but I am usually very tired after work.

Interviewer: That is understandable. How often do you cook your own meals?
Tom: I usually cook dinner at home from Monday to Thursday because it is healthy and cheap. But on Friday and the weekend, I usually go to a restaurant or order food online.

Interviewer: Interesting. How often do you travel outside the city?
Tom: I rarely travel. Maybe I travel once a year for a short vacation. I am always busy with my job, so I prefer to stay home and relax on my days off.

Interviewer: One last question, Tom. How often do you visit your family?
Tom: I usually visit my parents once a month. They live in a different city, so I have to take the train. The train is very fast, but the tickets are expensive.

Interviewer: Thank you for sharing your habits with us, Tom!
