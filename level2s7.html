6

Speaking Tasks

Practice stating extreme habits aloud with clear adverb placement.

Task A: Read Aloud

"I always wake up early and I always drink coffee."

Task B: Read Aloud

"I never smoke and I never drink alcohol."

Task C: Read Aloud

"He always studies hard, but he never passes the test."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"What do you always do in the morning?"
You:
"I always eat breakfast. What about you?"
Teacher:
"I never eat breakfast. I am not hungry."
You:
"Wow! I am always hungry in the morning."
7

Reading

Read the profile of a strict daily routine and answer the frequency questions.

John is a very healthy man with a strict routine. He always wakes up at 5:00 AM, Monday to Sunday. He never sleeps late. He always drinks water in the morning, but he never drinks coffee because he hates it.

He always exercises before work. He runs in the park. He never takes the bus to work; he always walks.

At night, John never watches television. He always reads a book for one hour. He always goes to bed at 9:00 PM. He never stays awake after 10:00 PM.

Questions:

1. Does John ever sleep late?
2. What does John never drink in the morning?
3. Does John take the bus to work?
4. What does John always do at night?
5. Does John stay awake after 10:00 PM?
