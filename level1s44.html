Speaking Tasks

"Who is he? He is my father. Who is she? She is my mother."

Task B: Read Aloud

"Where is the book? It is on the desk. It is not under the desk."

Task C: Read Aloud

"What is that? That is a car. Is it fast? Yes, it is very fast."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (Interrogation)
Teacher:
"Who is the boy next to you?"
You:
"He is my brother."
Teacher:
"Where is your bag?"
You:
"My bag is under the desk."
Teacher:
"What is in the bag?"

"A book and a pen are in the bag."

Reading

Read the story identifying people and places, then answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Lily. I am in the park today with my family. The park is a very big place. It has tall trees and green grass. Look at my family! Who is the tall man? He is my father. Where is he? He is under a big tree. He is very happy.

Who is the short woman? She is my mother. Where is she? She is next to my father. What is she doing? She is reading a book. The book is blue and it is very big.

Who are they? They are my brother and my sister. They are playing football. The football is black and white. It is very fast. Where are my brother and sister? They are on the green grass. They are running fast.

Where is my dog? My dog is not under the tree. My dog is not on the grass. Look! The dog is in a big box! It is very funny. The dog is black and small. It likes the box.

What is that in the sky? It is a bird. The bird is very small. I am happy in the park with my family. Who is in your family? Where are you today? What is near you?

1. Who is under the big tree?
2. What is the mother doing?
