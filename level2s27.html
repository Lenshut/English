Speaking Tasks

Practice playing 'This or That' using 'prefer' aloud.

Task A: Read Aloud

"I prefer coffee to tea, because coffee gives me energy."

Task B: Read Aloud

"She prefers cats to dogs because cats are very quiet."

Task C: Read Aloud

"We prefer summer to winter. We do not like cold weather."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (This or That)
Teacher:
"Let's play This or That! Do you prefer the city or the country?"
You:
"I prefer the city to the country. I like busy places."
Teacher:
"Okay! Do you prefer reading books or watching movies?"
You:
"I prefer watching movies to reading books. It is more fun."
7

Reading

Read the comparison article and identify the author's ultimate preferences.

City Life vs. Country Life

Many people love the city. The city has big restaurants, fast cars, and many jobs. However, I prefer the country to the city. The country is quiet and clean. I prefer walking in the forest to driving on busy roads.

In the morning, I prefer drinking coffee outside to sitting in a loud cafe. My brother disagrees with me. He prefers the city to the country because he likes to go to clubs at night.

But for me, I prefer nature to buildings. I think the country is the best place to live.

Questions:

1. Does the author prefer the country or the city?
2. What does the author prefer to driving on busy roads?
3. What does the author prefer to sitting in a loud cafe?
4. What does the author's brother prefer?
5. Does the author prefer nature or buildings?
