Speaking Tasks

Read the negative sentences out loud. Emphasize the word 'NOT'.

Task A: Read Aloud

"I am not a teacher. I am a student."

Task B: Read Aloud

"He is not a girl. He is a boy."

Task C: Correcting

"We are not boys. We are girls."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"Are you a teacher?"
You:
"No! I am not a teacher."
Teacher:
"(Points to a female student) Is she a boy?"
You:
"No! She is not a boy. She is a girl."
