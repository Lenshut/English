Speaking Tasks

Combine all Phase 2 skills out loud.

Task A: Object Show & Tell

"Look! This is a book. It is a blue book."

Task B: Commands

"Stand up, look at the book, and sit down."

Task C: Q & A

"Is this a red pen? No, it is not. It is a blue pen."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"Hello. Stand up, please."
You:
"(Stand up) Hello, Teacher."
Teacher:
"(Hold a red book) What is this?"
You:
"It is a red book."
Teacher:
"Is it green?"
You:
"No, it is not."
Teacher:
"Good. Sit down, please."

I sit on a blue chair.

Open the bag. What is inside?

I have a green pen.

I have a yellow pencil.

I have an eraser. Is it pink? No, it is white.

The teacher says, "Listen to me, please."

"Open the red book," says the teacher.

I open the book. I am a good student.
