Speaking Tasks

Practice recounting a fluid past narrative without slipping into the present tense.

Task A: Read Aloud

"Yesterday, I was at home. I cleaned my room and I watched a good movie."

Task B: Read Aloud

"We went to the restaurant, but we didn't eat pizza. We had pasta."

Task C: Read Aloud

"Did you have a good weekend? I went to the park, but I didn't see my friends."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (Weekend Catch-up)
Teacher:
"Hello! How was your weekend?"
You:
"It was very good. I relaxed at home."
Teacher:
"Did you go to the cinema?"
You:
"No, I didn't. I stayed home and I cooked a big dinner. Did you have a good weekend?"
7

Reading

Read the comprehensive weekend story and answer the questions.

Last weekend, my friend and I went to the beach. The weather was sunny and very hot. We were very happy.

We played volleyball and we swam in the sea. We didn't eat at a restaurant because we cooked our own food. We had sandwiches and apples. We didn't see any dolphins, but we saw a lot of birds.

In the evening, we were tired, so we went home early. We had a great time! Did you have a good weekend?

Questions:

1. Where did they go?
2. How was the weather?
3. What did they do at the beach?
4. Why didn't they eat at a restaurant?
5. Did they have a good time?
