Writing • Level 3

Pre-Intermediate Writing

Refine your narrative skills. Write detailed stories using past continuous, draft practical emails for real-world problems, and logically sequence events.

💡 10 Writing Topics

Choose a topic below and write a detailed paragraph (6-8 sentences) about it.

1. A Surprise: Write about a time you were surprised. Use past continuous (I was walking when...).
2. Travel Disaster: Describe a trip where things went wrong (flight delayed, lost bag).
3. Strong Opinion: State an opinion strongly (I strongly believe...) and give 2 examples.
4. 5-Year Plan: Use 'hope to' and 'plan to' to describe your life in 5 years.
5. An Apology: Write a formal apology to a friend for missing their birthday party.
6. Restaurant Review: Review a restaurant giving both good and bad points (..., but...).
7. Health Advice: A friend is very sick. Write 4 sentences of advice using 'should' and 'must'.
8. Childhood Habit: Explain something you 'used to' do but don't do anymore.
9. Sudden Twist: Write a short story that includes the word 'Suddenly' in the middle.
10. Returning an Item: Write a script explaining to a cashier why you are returning a broken phone.

📧 Email Drafting

Read the scenario and draft a short, professional email (3-5 sentences).

Scenario 1: You are late for work.

Write an email to your boss. Apologize, explain the reason (e.g., traffic, train delayed), and state what time you will arrive.

To: manager@company.com
Subject: Running Late

Scenario 2: Canceling a meeting.

Write to a client. Use "I meant to..." but explain why you can't make it. Propose a new time using "Are you free...".

To: client@business.com
Subject: Meeting Update

📑 Story Sequencing

The sentences below are out of order. Read them, then rewrite them in the correct narrative sequence into a single, cohesive paragraph using transition words (First, suddenly, luckily, in the end).

  • I found my passport under the bed.
  • I was packing my bag for my flight.
  • I arrived at the airport on time.
  • I realized my passport was missing.
