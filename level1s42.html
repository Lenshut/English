7

Reading

Read the story about David navigating the town to go shopping and answer the questions.

Hello! My name is David. I am a tourist in this big town. Today, I want to buy a new blue shirt and black pants. I am at the big park, but I do not know where the clothes store is. I see a tall man. I ask, "Excuse me, please. Where is the clothes store?" The tall man says, "Go straight on this street. Turn right at the big hospital. The store is next to the school. It is across from the tall trees."

I say, "Thank you very much!" The man says, "You're welcome." I walk fast. I go straight and I turn right at the big hospital. I see the school. Next to the school, there is a big store.

I go into the store. I see a nice blue shirt. I ask the nice woman, "Excuse me, how much is this shirt?" She says, "The shirt is ten dollars." Then I see black pants. I remember pants are plural, so I ask, "How much are these black pants?" She says, "The pants are twenty dollars."

I say, "I want the blue shirt and the black pants, please." I give the woman thirty dollars. She says, "Thank you!" I say, "You're welcome." I am very happy. I have new clothes, and I know the directions in this town now!

Questions:

1. Where is David at the start of the story?
2. Where is the clothes store?
3. How much is the blue shirt?
4. How much are the black pants?
5. Does David turn left or right at the hospital?
