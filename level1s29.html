7

Reading

Read the story about Emma's daily schedule and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Emma. I am a young student. Today, I want to tell you about my day. It is a very busy day. In the morning, I wake up early. I wake up at 6 o'clock. I am happy in the morning. I drink cold water and I eat hot food. At 7 o'clock, I read a small book in my room.

At 8 o'clock, I go to school. My school is a very big place. I study English and I read big books at school. My friend, Leo, is at school too. He is a tall boy. Leo and I study at 9 o'clock. We listen to the teacher. The teacher is a nice woman.

At 12 o'clock, it is time to eat. I eat an apple and a sandwich. I drink cold water. Leo eats food too. He eats a big sandwich. At 3 o'clock, I go home. I do not go to the park. I walk to my house.

My mother is at the house. She is a short woman. At 4 o'clock, my mother and I play with the dog. The dog is big and black. The dog runs fast. My mother plays with the fast dog in the garden.

At 6 o'clock, my father goes home. He goes to the house in a car. At 7 o'clock, we eat dinner. We eat hot food and we drink cold water. At 8 o'clock, I write a letter. I write in my room. At 9 o'clock, I am tired. I sleep in my big bed. I sleep at 9 o'clock every night. It is a very good day.

Questions:

1. What time does Emma wake up?
2. What time does she go to school?
3. What time is it time to eat?
4. What time does her father go home?
5. What time does Emma sleep?
