7

Reading

Read the story comparing Alex and his father's routine, and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Alex. I am a young boy and I am a student. Today, I want to tell you about my day and my father's day. My father is a tall man. His name is Mr. Green. We have very different routines.

In the morning, my father wakes up very early. He wakes up at 6 o'clock. He washes his face and he drinks hot coffee. I do not wake up at 6 o'clock. I wake up at 7 o'clock. I drink cold water and I eat an apple. At 8 o'clock, my father goes to work. He goes to work in a fast, black car. I do not go to work. I go to school. I walk to school with my friends.

In the afternoon, I am at school. I study English and I read books. My father is at work. He works in a big building. At 12 o'clock, we eat food. I eat a sandwich at school. My father eats hot food at work.

At 3 o'clock, I go home. I play in the park with my big dog. The dog runs fast. My father does not play in the park. He works. He goes home at 6 o'clock.

In the evening, we are happy. We eat dinner at 7 o'clock. We eat hot food and we drink cold water. At 9 o'clock, I am tired. I sleep in my big bed. My father reads a book. He sleeps at 10 o'clock. We have a very busy day!

Questions:

1. What time does Alex wake up?
2. What time does Mr. Green wake up?
3. Where does Alex go at 8 o'clock?
4. Does Mr. Green play in the park?
5. What time does Alex sleep?
