Speaking Tasks

Read the compound sentences out loud smoothly without pausing between the clauses.

Task A: Read Aloud

"I wake up at seven o'clock and I drink a glass of water."

Task B: Read Aloud

"My mother is a teacher and my father is a doctor."

Task C: Read Aloud

"I like to play football and I like to watch movies."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"Hello! What do you like to do on the weekend?"
You:
"Hi! I like to go to the park and I like to read books. What about you?"
Teacher:
"I like to sleep and I like to cook!"
You:
"That is great. I also like to cook and I like to eat."

Reading

Read the passage below and answer the questions using compound sentences where possible.

My name is Mark and I live in a big city. I wake up at seven o'clock in the morning and I drink a glass of water. I eat breakfast and I read the news. I like apples and I like bananas for breakfast.

I go to work and I sit at my desk. I am a doctor and I work in a hospital. The hospital is very big and it is very busy. I help people and I talk to the nurses.

In the evening, I go home and I cook dinner. I eat dinner and I watch television. I like to watch movies and I like to listen to music.

On the weekend, I go to the park and I play football. I meet my friends and we have fun. We eat pizza and we drink juice. I read a book and I go to sleep. I am happy and I am healthy.

Questions:

1. Where does Mark live?
2. What does Mark eat for breakfast?
3. Where does Mark work?
4. What does Mark do in the evening?
5. What do Mark and his friends eat on the weekend?
