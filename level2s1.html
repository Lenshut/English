7

Reading

Read the passage below and answer the questions identifying the linked parallel ideas.

My name is Mark, and I live in a very big city. I have a very busy life, and I want to tell you about my daily routine. Every morning, I wake up at six o'clock, and I immediately go to the kitchen. I drink a large glass of water, and I make a strong cup of black coffee. I like my coffee hot, and I like it strong. After breakfast, I take a quick shower, and I put on my suit. I take the train to work, and I read the news on my phone.

I work in a tall office building, and my job is very important. I am a manager, and I work with a lot of different people. I write emails in the morning, and I go to meetings in the afternoon. My coworkers are very smart, and they are very friendly. We work hard, and we help each other every day. At one o'clock, I take a break, and I eat lunch with my friends. I usually eat a healthy salad, and I drink sparkling water.

In the evening, I finish my work, and I leave the office. I go to the gym, and I exercise for one hour. I run on the treadmill, and I lift heavy weights. It is very tiring, and it makes me sweat, but it is good for my health. After the gym, I walk back to my apartment, and I cook a delicious dinner. I listen to classical music, and I relax on my comfortable sofa. Sometimes, I call my parents, and I tell them about my day. Finally, I brush my teeth, and I go to sleep at ten o'clock. I sleep very deeply, and I dream about my next vacation.

Questions:

1. What two things does Mark do in the kitchen every morning?
2. What two things does Mark do when he travels to work?
3. What two things does he do at his job?
4. What two things does he do at the gym?
5. What two things does he do to relax at home in the evening?
