Writing • Level 1

Foundation Writing

Develop basic literacy. Practice forming words, spelling correctly, organizing simple sentences, and filling out basic personal information.

💡 10 Writing Topics

Choose a topic below and write 3-5 simple sentences about it.

1. Myself: Write your name, age, and how you feel today.
2. My Family: Who is in your family? (He is my father, she is...)
3. Colors: Write about your 3 favorite colors and objects that match.
4. My Classroom: Describe 3 things in this room using colors.
5. Food I Like: List 3 foods you like and 1 food you do not like.
6. My Morning: Write 3 things you do when you wake up.
7. My Bag: What is in your bag? (I have two pens...)
8. Animals: Write about an animal. Is it big or small? What color is it?
9. My Friend: Describe a friend. Are they tall? Are they happy?
10. Where is it?: Describe where things are on your desk (in, on, under).
