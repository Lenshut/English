7

Reading

Read the short story and answer the questions below.

Hello. My name is Leo. I am a student. Look at my room. It is a big room. I have a box in my room. What is this? This is my box. It is a big box. I look in the box. What is this? This is a pen. The pen is red. What is this? This is a book. The book is blue. The pen and the book are in the box. I hold the book. I am happy.

Look! What is that? I point out the window. That is a tall tree. The tree is very big. What is that? That is a bird. The bird is very small. The bird is in the tall tree. What is that? That is a car. The car is fast. It is not slow. It is a red car. The car is on the street.

I hear a dog. Where is the dog? Look out the window. What is that? That is my dog! The dog is big and fast. The dog is under the tall tree. Who is that? That is my brother. My brother is short. He plays with the big dog. The dog is very fast, but my brother is fast too.

Today is a very hot day. The sun is hot. I am in my house, and I am not hot. The house is good. I look at my hand. What is this? This is a glass of water. The water is cold. I drink the cold water. I like my room. I like the big tree and the small bird. What is that in the sky? That is the sun. It is a very happy day.

Questions:

1. What is in Leo's box?
2. What is tall out the window?
3. Who plays with the dog?
4. Is the brother tall or short?
5. Is the water hot or cold?
