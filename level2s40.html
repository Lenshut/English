Speaking Tasks

Practice delivering a clear, polite complaint to a manager aloud.

Task A: Read Aloud

"Excuse me, I have a problem. The TV in my room does not work."

Task B: Read Aloud

"Hello, my room is very dirty, and the towels are missing."

Task C: Read Aloud

"Excuse me, the air conditioning does not work, and the window is broken."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (Hotel Reception)
Reception:
"Hello, how can I help you today?"
You:
"Excuse me, I have a problem with my room. The Wi-Fi does not work."
Reception:
"I am so sorry. I will fix that. Is everything else okay?"
You:
"No, the shower does not work, and the towels are missing."
7

Reading

Read the hotel complaint email and identify the functional and physical issues.

To: Hotel Manager
Subject: Problems in Room 405

Dear Manager,

I am staying in Room 405 and I have many problems. First, the room is very dirty. The bed sheets are dirty, and the soap in the bathroom is missing.

Also, the electronics are bad. The television does not work, and the air conditioning is broken. It is very hot in here!

Finally, I cannot open the window because the handle is missing. Please fix these problems or give me a new room.

Thank you,
David

Questions:

1. What is wrong with the bed sheets?
2. What is missing in the bathroom?
3. What is the problem with the television?
4. What is the problem with the air conditioning?
5. Why can't David open the window?
