Pre-Intermediate Reading

Test your narrative comprehension. Practice reading past continuous stories, identifying first conditional logic, deducing vocabulary, and summarizing main ideas.

📖 Read & Answer

"Yesterday morning, I was walking to the train station when I suddenly realized I had left my phone at home. I was very worried because my train ticket was on the phone. I ran back quickly. Luckily, I found it on the kitchen table. In the end, I caught my train, but it was a stressful morning."

✅ True or False

"If you travel to London, you should definitely visit the British Museum. It is huge, so you might not see everything in one day. However, it is completely free to enter! You must not touch the ancient artifacts, but you can take as many photos as you want. I strongly believe it is the best museum in the city."
1. The author thinks you shouldn't visit the museum.
2. It costs money to go inside the museum.
3. You are allowed to take photos.
4. It is a strict rule that you cannot touch the artifacts.
5. You can easily see everything in the museum in a few hours.

🔍 Vocabulary in Context

Read the sentence and guess the meaning of the underlined word based on the context.

1. I was driving slowly, but suddenly a deer jumped in front of my car!

2. I strongly believe that public transport should be free for everyone.

3. If it rains, I will probably stay home, but I am not certain yet.

4. You lost your passport? You should go to the embassy immediately.

5. I intended to call you yesterday, but I fell asleep.

📑 Sequencing

Read the sentences. Put them in the correct narrative order (1, 2, 3, 4) using the transition words as clues.

A. Luckily, a friendly man saw me and helped me change the tire.
B. Suddenly, I heard a loud pop and my car started shaking.
C. In the end, I arrived at work, but I was 30 minutes late.
D. Yesterday morning, I was driving to work on the highway.

💡 Inference

"I see your point about living in a big city. It is definitely more exciting, and there are many jobs. For example, my brother moved to Tokyo and found a great career. However, I prefer the countryside. What I mean is, I need a quiet place to relax. If I lived in a noisy city, I would be too stressed to work well."
