Speaking Tasks

"Stand up, please. Sit down, please."

Task B: Read Aloud & Act

"Open the book. Close the book."

Task C: Read Aloud

"Look at the board and listen to the teacher."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (Simon Says)
Teacher:
"Listen to me! Stand up, please."
You:
"(Student stands up)"
Teacher:
"Open the book."
You:
"(Student opens the book)"
Teacher:
"Now it is your turn. Command the teacher!"
You:
"Sit down, please."

Reading

Read the classroom instructions and answer the questions.

Teacher: Hello, class! Please, sit down.

Class: Hello, Teacher.

Teacher: Look at the board. What color is it?

Class: It is green!

Teacher: Yes, it is. Now, open the book.

Look at the book.

Listen to me.

"A is for Apple. B is for Boy."

OK, close the book.

Stand up, please.

Open the door.

Goodbye, class!
