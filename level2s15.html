Speaking Tasks

Practice recounting an event using irregular verbs aloud.

Task A: Read Aloud

"I went to the market and I had a sandwich."

Task B: Read Aloud

"She ate an apple and she saw a dog."

Task C: Read Aloud

"We went home because we had a lot of work."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"Tell me about your weekend. Where did you go?"
You:
"I went to the restaurant with my friend."
Teacher:
"That sounds nice. What did you eat?"
You:
"We ate pizza and we had a great time!"
7

Reading

Read a recount of a trip and identify the irregular past verbs used.

Last Saturday, my family and I went to the city. We had a plan to visit the big museum.

In the morning, we ate a quick breakfast at home. Then, we went to the train station. On the train, I saw many interesting buildings outside the window.

When we arrived, we went straight to the museum. We saw old dinosaur bones and beautiful paintings. After the museum, we were very hungry. We went to a famous restaurant and we ate pasta. We had a fantastic day together.

Questions (Answer with complete sentences using the bolded verbs):

1. Where did the family go on Saturday?
2. What did they eat in the morning?
3. What did the writer see on the train?
4. What did they see at the museum?
5. What kind of day did they have?
