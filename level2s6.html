Speaking Tasks

Review: Fluidly mix all conjunctions in natural spoken paragraphs.

"I like to go to the park and I like to read, but I do not like sports."

Task B: Read Aloud

"I am learning English because I want to travel, so I study every day."

Task C: Read Aloud

"My house is small but it is beautiful, and I love it."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"Let's practice for the interview! Do you like sports?"
You:
"Yes, I like soccer and tennis, but I do not like basketball."
Teacher:
"Why do you like soccer?"
You:
"I like soccer because it is fast, so I play it every Saturday."

Reading

Read the passage below and answer the questions tracking the complex conjunction logic.

Anna has a very busy life. She works in an office and she studies at night, but she is happy because she likes her job.

On Saturday, she wants to relax, so she stays home and she reads books. She likes to read because it is quiet. She does not like loud places, so she never goes to clubs.

Anna likes coffee but she does not drink it at night because she wants to sleep. She drinks tea instead, so she sleeps very well.

Questions:

1. What two things does Anna do in her busy life?
2. Why is Anna happy?
3. She wants to relax on Saturday, so what does she do?
4. Why does she never go to clubs?
5. She drinks tea instead of coffee at night, so what is the result?
