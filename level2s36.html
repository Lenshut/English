Short Answer

Write a brief paragraph ranking three different items (e.g., three foods, three cities) from good to best or bad to worst.

Example: "Apples are good. Bananas are better than apples. But pizza is the best food."

1. Rank three types of food (good -> better -> best).

"Please tell us about your three favorite movies."

"I like 'Spiderman', it is very exciting. I think 'Batman' is more exciting than 'Spiderman'."

Tech Review: Smartphones of 2026

Today, we review three phones: The Alpha, The Beta, and The Omega.

The Alpha is a good phone. It is fast and light. The Beta is better than the Alpha because it has a longer battery life. However, the Omega is the best phone of the year. It is the fastest and the most beautiful.

Let's talk about price. The Alpha is expensive. The Beta is more expensive than the Alpha. But the Omega is the most expensive phone in the store. If you want a cheap phone, the Alpha is the cheapest. If you want the most powerful phone, buy the Omega.

Questions:

1. Why is the Beta better than the Alpha?
2. Which phone is the best of the year?
3. Is the Beta more expensive than the Omega?
4. Which phone is the most expensive?
