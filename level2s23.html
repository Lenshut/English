Task C: Read Aloud

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (The Deadline)
Boss:
"When will you finish the new project?"
You:
"I will finish it next week."
Boss:
"Next week is too late! Can you do it in two days?"
You:
"Yes, I can. I will give it to you in two days."

Reading

Read the project schedule and map the future events to a timeline.

Memo: Office Relocation Plan

Hello team, we are going to move to our new office very soon. Here is the schedule:

Tomorrow: The moving company will bring empty boxes to our current office. Please start packing your desks.
In two days: We are going to pack all the computers and electronics.
Next week: The trucks will take all the boxes to the new building. We will work from home during this time.
Next month: We are going to have a big party in the new office to celebrate!
Next year: We will hire ten new people to join our bigger team.

Questions (When does this happen?):

1. When will the moving company bring boxes?
2. When are they going to pack computers?
3. When will the trucks take the boxes?
4. When are they going to have a party?
5. When will they hire new people?
