7

Reading

Read the story about Sam visiting places in his town and answer the questions.

Hello. My name is Sam. Today is a very busy day. I go to many places in my town. In the morning, I wake up and eat breakfast. At 8 o'clock, I go to the school. I am a student. I learn English and I read books at the school. My sister goes to the school too. My father is a doctor. He goes to the hospital. He helps people at the big hospital. He works very hard.

In the afternoon, I go to the store with my mother. The store is very big. We buy food. We buy red apples, yellow bananas, and white bread. I ask the man, 'How much is the bread?' The bread is two dollars. We pay the money.

After the store, I go to the park. I meet my friends in the park. We play football and we run fast. The park has tall trees and green grass. I like the park. My big dog plays in the park too.

In the evening, we go to the house. The house is a very good place. We eat the food from the store. We are tired. We sleep in our beds. I like my town.

Questions:

1. Where does Sam go at 8 o'clock?
2. Where does Sam's father work?
3. What do they buy at the store?
4. Where do Sam and his friends play football?
5. Where do they go in the evening?
