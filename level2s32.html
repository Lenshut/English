Speaking Tasks

Practice making value judgments aloud using long adjectives.

Task A: Read Aloud

"This gold watch is more expensive than the silver watch."

Task B: Read Aloud

"I think reading books is more interesting than watching TV."

Task C: Read Aloud

"A snake is more dangerous than a dog, but a lion is more dangerous than a snake."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (Product Pitch)
Customer:
"I need a new phone. This cheap phone is okay..."
You (Seller):
"No, don't buy that! This luxury phone is more beautiful."
Customer:
"But the luxury phone is $1,000! It is very expensive."
You:
"Yes, it is more expensive, but it is also more intelligent."

Reading

Read the travel brochure and answer the questions based on the long adjective comparisons.

Travel Guide: Rome vs. London

Are you planning your next vacation? Rome and London are both great cities, but they are very different.

Rome is older than London, and many people think it is more beautiful because of the ancient buildings. However, London is a very modern city. London is more expensive than Rome. Food and hotels cost a lot of money in London.

If you like history, Rome is more interesting. But if you like shopping and big shows, London is more exciting. The flight to London is longer, so a flight to Rome is more comfortable for people in Europe.

Questions:

1. Why do many people think Rome is more beautiful?
2. Which city is more expensive?
3. Which city is more interesting for history?
4. Which city is more exciting for shopping?
5. Which flight is more comfortable for people in Europe?
