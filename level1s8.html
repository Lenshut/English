7

Reading

Read the dialogue below and answer the Yes/No questions.

Teacher: Hello, John. Look at my hand. Is this a pen?

John: No, it is not. It is an eraser.

Teacher: Good! Now, look at the wall. Is this a window?

John: Yes, it is.

Teacher: Look at this. Is this a bag?

John: No, it is not. It is a book.

Teacher: Very good. Is this a door?

John: Yes, it is a door.

Teacher: And what is this?

John: It is a bag.

Questions:

1. Does the teacher have a pen in her hand?
2. What is in the teacher's hand? (It is an...)
3. Are they looking at a window?
4. Is the third object a bag?
5. Is the fourth object a door?
