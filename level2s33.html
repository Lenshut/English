Speaking Tasks

Task B: Read Aloud

"Losing your passport is much worse than losing your umbrella."

Task C: Read Aloud

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (Debating Quality)
Teacher:
"I think fast food is better than home cooking. Do you agree?"
You:
"I disagree. Home cooking is much better than fast food!"
Teacher:
"But fast food is cheap. That is good."
You:
"Yes, but being unhealthy is worse than spending money."

Reading

Read the editorial review comparing two options and answer the questions.

Restaurant Review: Burger Palace vs. The Green Bowl

Today, I visited two new restaurants in the city. First, I went to Burger Palace. The food there is good, and it is very cheap. However, if you want to be healthy, The Green Bowl is better. The Green Bowl uses fresh vegetables and clean ingredients.

The service at The Green Bowl is bad. The waiters are very slow. But the service at Burger Palace is even worse! They forgot my drink and my food was cold.

Overall, Burger Palace is faster, but The Green Bowl is better for your body. Next time, I will eat at home. It is better than both of them!

Questions:

1. Is the food at Burger Palace good or bad?
2. Which restaurant is better for your health?
3. Is the service at The Green Bowl good?
4. Which restaurant has worse service?
5. What does the writer think is better than both restaurants?
