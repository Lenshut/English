6

Speaking Tasks

Practice aggressively denying false past accusations aloud.

Task A: Read Aloud (Denial)

"No! I did not steal the car! I did not go there!"

Task B: Read Aloud (Correction)

"I did not eat your sandwich. I ate an apple."

Task C: Read Aloud (Facts)

"She did not call me yesterday, and she did not send a message."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (Interrogation)
Police:
"You went to the bank yesterday! You took the money!"
You:
"No! I did not go to the bank. I did not take the money."
Police:
"You saw the manager! You talked to him!"
You:
"I did not see the manager, and I did not talk to him. I was at home!"
