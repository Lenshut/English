Comprehensive Reading

Read this letter from a student reflecting on their life. Answer the final reading comprehension questions.

Dear Diary,

Today was the last day of my English class. Last year, I didn't know any grammar and I was very nervous, but today I am the most confident student in the room. I think learning a language is the hardest thing to do, but it is also the best because you can talk to people from all over the world.

I usually practice every day. Sometimes it is tiring, so I watch movies in English instead of reading books. Tomorrow, I am going to have a big party with my classmates to celebrate.

Next year, I will travel to London. I bought the tickets yesterday! I am very excited. I think my future is going to be amazing.

Questions:

1. How did the student feel last year?
2. Why do they think learning a language is "the best"?
3. What do they do instead of reading books when they are tired?
4. What are they going to do tomorrow?
5. What is their plan for next year?
