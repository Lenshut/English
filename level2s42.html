Task D: Teacher Roleplay (The Party Guest)
Teacher:
"Hi there! What do you think about the food at this party?"
You:
"I think it is very good. I had the chicken. What about you?"
Teacher:
"I wanted the chicken, but I am vegetarian. I asked for pasta without meat."
You:
"Wow! Is the pasta good? Are you free to get lunch with me next week?"

Reading

Read the narrative about a dinner party and answer the conversational flow questions.

The Dinner Party

Last night, I went to a dinner party. I met a man named David. I asked, "What do you do?" He said he was an engineer, and then asked, "What about you?" I told him I was a teacher.

Later, we ordered food. David asked for a burger, but without cheese. I wanted soup, but the waiter said the stove did not work. So, I asked for a salad instead of soup.

After dinner, David asked, "What do you think about the music here?" I said it was too loud. Before I left, David asked, "Are you free on Saturday? Do you want to go to the park?" I said yes, and we made a plan!

Questions:

1. How did David return the question about his job?
2. What modification did David make to his burger?
3. Why couldn't the narrator have soup?
4. How did David ask for an opinion about the environment?
5. How did David invite the narrator to hang out?
