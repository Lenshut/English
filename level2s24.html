"What are your plans for the next 5 years?"

"I am going to move to London next year. I have the plane tickets."
Teacher:
"That's a great plan! What are your dreams for the future?"

"I think I will be very rich, and I will buy a big house with a dog."
7

Reading

Read the '5-Year Plan' essay and distinguish between plans and dreams.

My 5-Year Plan by Alex

In the next five years, my life is going to change a lot. Next year, I am going to move to New York. I found a small apartment there. I am going to study business at the university. This is a solid plan because I already paid for the classes.

But I also have many dreams. After university, I think I will start my own company. I don't know what kind of company yet, but I will think of something good. Maybe one day, I will buy a big house in the mountains.

I promise I will work very hard to make my dreams come true.

Questions:

1. Where is Alex going to move next year?
2. What is he going to study at university?
3. What does he think he will do after university?
4. What does he think he will buy one day?
5. What does he promise to do?
