LEVEL 4 : INTERMEDIATE

Program Overview

Welcome to Level 4. This program bridges the gap between casual conversation and structured, semi-professional discourse. Students will master complex cause-and-effect relationships, hypothetical conditions, debate structures, and formal presentation skills.

Skill Progression Path

Skill expansion at Start (Ph 1), Midpoint (Ph 4), and Goal (Ph 8).

🏆 Master Goal

Speak confidently in structured paragraphs (6–10 sentences). Explain abstract ideas logically, debate controversial topics with clear counter-arguments, resolve complex real-life problems, and deliver basic professional presentations.
