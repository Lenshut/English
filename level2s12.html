Reading

Read the mixed frequency text and correct the false statements below.

Welcome to the "Healthy Habits" magazine! Today, we are interviewing two famous athletes, Michael and Sarah, to learn about their daily routines. They are both Olympic champions, but their habits are completely different.

Interviewer: Michael, how often do you train?
Michael: I always train six days a week. I usually wake up at 4:00 AM, and I immediately go to the swimming pool. I am always energetic in the morning, so I never feel tired. However, I sometimes take a nap in the afternoon because my body needs to recover.

Interviewer: That is incredible! Sarah, what about you? How often do you wake up at 4:00 AM?
Sarah: I never wake up that early! I am usually very sleepy in the morning. I am a gymnast, so I usually train in the late afternoon or evening. I always sleep for nine hours every night because sleep is very important for my muscles. I am never late for my evening training sessions.

Interviewer: Let's talk about food. Michael, how often do you eat fast food?
Michael: I never eat fast food. I always cook my own meals. I usually eat chicken, rice, and a lot of green vegetables. I am always careful about my diet.

Interviewer: And you, Sarah?
Sarah: I usually eat very healthy food, but I sometimes eat a slice of chocolate cake on the weekend. I think it is important to enjoy life! I always drink three liters of water every day, but I never drink sugary sodas.

Interviewer: Thank you both! It is very interesting to see that champions have different schedules. Michael is always an early bird, and Sarah is usually a night owl, but they are both amazing athletes.

Questions (Based on the text):

1. How often does Michael wake up at 4:00 AM?
2. Why does Sarah always sleep for nine hours every night?
3. How often does Michael eat fast food?
4. What does Sarah sometimes eat on the weekend?
5. Who is an early bird, and who is a night owl?
