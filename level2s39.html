Reading

Read the complex cafe order below and list the specific modifications requested.

Order Ticket #402

Customer: Hello, I would like to order lunch for two people. First, I want a large coffee. But I am allergic to milk, so coffee without milk, please. I want extra sugar in it.

Customer: Also, I want a chicken sandwich. But please, no mayonnaise on the sandwich. My friend wants a pizza. Can she have extra cheese on the pizza?

Customer: Wait, actually, I don't want the chicken sandwich. I will take the pasta instead of the sandwich. Thank you.

Questions (What are the modifications?):

1. What is removed from the coffee?
2. What is added to the coffee?
3. What is removed from the sandwich?
4. What is added to the pizza?
5. What does the customer order instead of the sandwich at the end?
