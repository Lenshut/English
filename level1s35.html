7

Reading

Read the story about Anna using polite words and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Anna. I am a student. I am a very polite girl. I like to be nice to my family and my friends. In the morning, I wake up at seven o'clock. I go to the kitchen. My mother is in the kitchen. She cooks hot food. I say, "Good morning, mother. I want bread and water, please." My mother gives me the bread and water. I smile and say, "Thank you, mother!" My mother smiles and says, "You're welcome, Anna."

At eight o'clock, I go to school. I walk into the big classroom. I see my teacher, Mr. Smith. He is a tall man. I say, "Excuse me, Mr. Smith. Where is my book?" Mr. Smith gives me my big blue book. I say, "Thank you, teacher." He says, "You're welcome." I sit at my desk and I read the book.

In the afternoon, I go to the park with my brother, Tom. It is a very hot day. Tom is hot and tired. He says, "Anna, I want cold water, please." I have cold water in my bag. I give the cold water to Tom. Tom drinks the cold water and says, "Thank you, Anna!" I say, "You're welcome, Tom." We play in the park and we are very happy. It is always good to say please, thank you, and excuse me. Polite words make people happy every day!

Questions:

1. What does Anna say to her mother in the kitchen?
2. What does Anna say when the teacher gives her the book?
3. What does Mr. Smith say to Anna?
4. What does Tom want in the park?
5. What are the three polite words in the story?
