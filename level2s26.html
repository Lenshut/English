Speaking Tasks

Practice vocalizing your agreement or disagreement aloud.

"I completely agree with you. It is a great idea."

Task B: Read Aloud

"I am sorry, but I disagree with you. I think it is wrong."

Task C: Read Aloud

"I do not agree with him, but I agree with her."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (Agree or Disagree?)
Teacher:
"I think we should ban cars in the city center. Do you agree?"
You:
"I agree with you! The city is too loud and the air is bad."
Teacher:
"I also think people should work 6 days a week."
You:
"I disagree with you! People need time to rest with their families."
