Task D: Teacher Roleplay (The Favor Matrix)
Teacher:
"Excuse me, I am very thirsty. Will you buy me a coffee?"
You:
"No, I won't buy you a coffee. I don't have money."
Teacher:
"Okay, I understand. Will you lend me your pen?"
You:
"Yes, I will. Here is my pen."
7

Reading

Read the text exchange focusing on future favors and refusals.

Tom: Hey Lisa! Are you at home?
Lisa: Yes, I am. I am studying for my English test.
Tom: Oh, okay. Will you help me move some boxes? My car is full.
Lisa: No, I won't. I am too busy right now. Sorry!
Tom: That's okay. Will you lend me your big bag, then?
Lisa: No, I won't lend you my bag. I need it tomorrow for my trip.
Tom: Wow, okay. Will you at least come to my party on Saturday?
Lisa: Yes, I will come to your party! I will bring pizza.

Questions:

1. What does Tom ask Lisa to do first?
2. Will Lisa help Tom move boxes? Why not?
3. Will Lisa lend Tom her bag?
4. Why won't Lisa lend him the bag?
5. Will Lisa go to the party? What will she bring?
