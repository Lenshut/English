7

Reading

Read the story about a family dinner and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Emma. I am a happy girl. Today, I want to talk about food. Food is very good. I like food. In my family, we like different food. I like fruit. I like apples and I like bananas. I eat red apples in the morning. I eat yellow bananas in the afternoon. Fruit is very sweet and good.

My brother is a tall boy. His name is Leo. Leo does not like fruit. He likes meat. He likes hot meat for dinner. He eats meat and he drinks cold water. My father is a big man. He likes rice and he likes meat. He eats a big dinner. He eats hot rice and hot meat.

My mother is a nice woman. She likes fish and she likes rice. We eat dinner in our house. We sit at a big table. The table is in the kitchen. I eat my apples, Leo eats his meat, my father eats meat and rice, and my mother eats fish. We drink water. We like our food.

After dinner, we are very happy. We talk and we smile. What food do you like? Do you like apples or do you like meat? Do you like hot food or do you like cold food? I like my sweet apples.

Questions:

1. What food does Emma like?
2. What food does Leo like?
3. What food does the father like?
4. Where do they eat dinner?
5. Does Leo like fruit?
