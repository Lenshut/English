"What do you usually eat for breakfast?"

7

Reading

Read the profile of a flexible daily routine and answer the frequency questions.

Lisa is a freelance graphic designer who works from home, so her schedule is very flexible. She doesn't have a strict routine like an office worker. She usually wakes up around 8:00 AM, but sometimes she sleeps until 9:30 AM if she worked very late the night before. She usually eats a healthy bowl of oatmeal for breakfast, but sometimes she buys a sweet chocolate pastry from the bakery across the street when she wants a special treat.

Because she works from home, she usually wears comfortable sweatpants and a simple t-shirt during the day. However, she sometimes has important video meetings with her clients. When she has a meeting, she usually wears a professional blouse and fixes her hair nicely. She usually works at her kitchen table, but sometimes she goes to a local café to work because she wants a change of scenery and a fresh cup of coffee. She usually drinks two cups of coffee a day, but sometimes she drinks green tea in the afternoon.

In the evenings, Lisa usually cooks dinner for herself. She enjoys making pasta or grilling chicken. But sometimes, when she is too tired from looking at her computer screen all day, she orders pizza or sushi from a delivery app. After dinner, she usually reads a novel or listens to a podcast to relax her eyes. Sometimes she watches a movie, but not very often.

Her weekends are completely different from her weekdays. On Saturdays, she usually meets her best friend, Sarah. They usually go for a long walk in the city park or visit an art museum. Sometimes they take a short train ride to the beach if the weather is warm and sunny. On Sundays, Lisa usually cleans her apartment and prepares her schedule for the next week. She sometimes visits her parents for a traditional Sunday dinner, but usually, she prefers to stay home and prepare for Monday.

Questions:

1. What time does Lisa usually wake up, and when does she sometimes sleep late?
2. What does she usually wear, and what does she wear for video meetings?
3. Where does she usually work, and where does she sometimes go?
4. What does she usually do after dinner to relax her eyes?
5. Who does she usually meet on Saturdays, and what do they do?
