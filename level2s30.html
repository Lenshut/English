Task C: Teacher Roleplay (The Great Debate)
Teacher:
"I think fast food is better than cooking at home because it is very fast. What do you think?"
You:
"I completely disagree with you. I prefer cooking at home to fast food."
Teacher:
"Really? Why do you prefer cooking at home?"
You:
"Because cooking at home is very healthy, and it is cheaper."
7

Reading

Read the debate transcript and answer questions about the opposing viewpoints.

Debate Topic: Paper Books vs E-Books

Maria: What do you think about e-books? I think e-books are fantastic. I prefer e-books to paper books because they are very light. You can carry a hundred books in your bag.

John: I disagree with you, Maria. I do not think e-books are better. I prefer paper books because I like the smell of the paper, and I think it is better for your eyes. E-books make my eyes tired.

Maria: I understand, but I disagree. I think e-books are cheaper, so they are better for students.

John: That is true, they are cheaper. I agree with you about the price, but I still prefer paper books.

Questions:

1. What does Maria prefer?
2. Why does Maria prefer e-books (first reason)?
3. Does John agree with Maria? What does he prefer?
4. Why does John prefer paper books?
5. What is one thing John agrees with Maria about?
