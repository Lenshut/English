7

Reading

Read the story synthesizing Phase 1-4 concepts and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Mark. I am a student. I am ten years old. I am a tall boy, and I am very happy today. Look at my family. Who is the tall man? He is my father. My father is very tall and big. He is not short. Who is the short woman? She is my mother. My mother is short and nice. Who is the small girl? She is my sister. My sister is young and she is very fast. We are a happy family.

Look at my house. Where is my house? It is next to a big park. My house is not small. It is a very big house. In my house, my bag is on the desk. My book is in the bag. The dog is under the table. The dog is big and black. The dog is very fast. It is not a slow dog.

Today is a hot day. The sun is in the sky. The sun is very hot. I am hot, so I want cold water. I drink the cold water and I am good. I do not like cold days, but I like cold water. What about you? Are you a student? Are you tall or short? Are you happy today? Where are you? Are you in a big house or a small house? It is nice to meet you!

1. How old is Mark?
2. Is Mark's father tall or short?
3. Where is the big, black dog?
4. Is the house big or small?
5. What does Mark want on a hot day?
