LEVEL 3 : PRE-INTERMEDIATE

Program Overview

Welcome to Level 3. This program shifts students from basic interactions into continuous, multi-turn dialogues. Students will master storytelling, giving supported opinions, hypothetical situations, and navigating complex real-world travel and shopping issues.

Skill Progression Path

Skill expansion at Start (Ph 1), Midpoint (Ph 4), and Goal (Ph 8).

🏆 Master Goal

Speak fluidly in 4–6 sentence blocks. Tell detailed past stories, express opinions backed by concrete examples, give advice using modals, and handle real-life friction (complaints, apologies, making arrangements) completely in English.
