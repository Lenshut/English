7

Reading

Read the dialogue below and answer the questions.

Clerk: Hello. What is your name?

Man: Hi. My name is Tom.

Clerk: How do you spell your name?

Man: T-O-M.

Clerk: Thank you, Tom. And you? What is your name?

Woman: Hello. My name is Mary.

Clerk: How do you spell your name, Mary?

Woman: M-A-R-Y.

Clerk: Thank you, Mary. How old are you?

Woman: I am thirty years old.

Man: I am thirty-two years old.

Clerk: OK. Thank you, T-O-M and M-A-R-Y.

Questions:

1. What is the man's name?
2. How do you spell his name?
3. What is the woman's name?
4. How do you spell her name?
5. How old is the woman?
