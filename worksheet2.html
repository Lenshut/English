🗣️ Speaking Tasks

Practice these aloud with a partner or teacher.

01

Tell your partner what you did last weekend.

02

Ask your partner 3 questions starting with 'Did you...?'

03

Compare your hometown to another city using comparatives (-er / more).

04

Tell the class your 5-year life plan using 'Going to' and 'Hope to'.

05

State a strong opinion about food and give two reasons why.

06

Ask your partner how often they exercise, cook, and read.

07

Roleplay: Invite your partner to the cinema tomorrow.

08

Describe the best restaurant you have ever visited using superlatives.

09

Explain why you are studying English using 'because' and 'so'.

10

Have a 1-minute conversation and use 'What about you?' at least twice.
