Speaking Tasks

Practice describing past locations and states aloud.

Task A: Read Aloud

"I was at home yesterday, and I was very tired."

Task B: Read Aloud

"We were in London last year. It was beautiful."

Task C: Read Aloud

"They were late for school because the bus was slow."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"Where were you yesterday morning?"
You:
"I was at the supermarket. I was hungry."
Teacher:
"Were you at the park in the afternoon?"
You:
"No, I was at home. The weather was cold."

Reading

Read the historical description and distinguish between past states.

Last year, my family and I were in Rome. The city was very beautiful and old. The food was delicious, but it was expensive. My parents were very happy because the weather was hot and sunny.

The hotel was small, but the people were nice. We were at the Colosseum on Tuesday, and it was very crowded.

Yesterday, I looked at the photos. I was sad because I want to go back.

Questions:

1. Where were the family last year?
2. How was the city?
3. How was the food?
4. Why were the parents happy?
5. How was the hotel?
