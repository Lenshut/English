Reading

Read the detailed itinerary and outline the concrete future plans.

My Travel Itinerary to London

Next week, I am going to travel to London. I am very excited. My flight is on Monday. I am going to leave the house at 6:00 AM because the flight is early.

On Tuesday, I am going to visit the British Museum. I have the tickets on my phone. Then, I am going to eat dinner with my friend, Mark. We are going to a famous restaurant.

On Wednesday, I am going to buy some souvenirs. I am going to buy a small bus for my nephew. I am going to return home on Thursday evening.

4. Who are they going to eat dinner with?
