Reading

Read the text about world records and answer the superlative questions.

World Records

Our world is full of extremes! Let's look at some records. The blue whale is the biggest animal in the world. It is bigger than an elephant. The cheetah is the fastest animal on land. It is very fast!

Mount Everest is the highest mountain on Earth. Many people want to climb it, but it is very hard. The Sahara Desert is very hot, but Antarctica is the coldest place in the world.

Finally, the giraffe is tall, but is it the tallest animal? Wait, yes it is! The giraffe is the tallest animal in the world.
