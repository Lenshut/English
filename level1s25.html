7

Reading

Read the story and answer the questions below.

Hello. My name is Sam. I am a tall boy. I sleep in a big bed. The bed is in my room. At night, I sleep. I sleep in the big bed.

In the morning, I wake up. I wake up and I am very happy. I look out the window. The sun is in the sky. The sun is hot and big. It is a good day.

I walk to the kitchen. I am hot, so I want water. I drink water. I drink cold water. The cold water is very good. I drink the cold water in the kitchen.

I am a big boy, so I eat food. I eat hot food in the morning. My mother is in the kitchen. She is a nice woman. My mother eats food too. She eats hot food and she drinks tea. The tea is very hot. My father is in the kitchen too. He drinks cold water.

I have a dog. The dog is big and black. The dog wakes up in the morning. The dog eats food. The dog drinks water. The dog does not drink hot tea! The dog drinks cold water.

After I eat and drink, I am very happy. I like the morning. I like the hot sun and the cold water. I like my big dog. I do not sleep in the morning. I sleep at night. Now, I am ready for the day.

Questions:

1. Where does Sam sleep?
2. What does Sam do in the morning?
3. Does Sam drink hot water or cold water?
4. What does his mother drink?
5. Does the dog drink hot tea?
