Reading

Read the city survey and analyze the open-ended questions and answers.

City Survey: A New Park

Interviewer: Hello! We are asking people about the changes in the city. What do you think about the new park?
Citizen 1: I think it is beautiful. I love the big trees and the walking paths.

Interviewer: That is great. What do you think about the location?
Citizen 2: I do not think it is good. It is too far from my house, so I cannot walk there.

Interviewer: I see. What do you think about the new parking rules near the park?
Citizen 1: I think they are very expensive. Many people will not visit because they cannot pay for parking.
Citizen 2: I agree with her. I think it is a big problem.

Questions:

1. What is the first question the interviewer asks?
2. What does Citizen 1 think about the new park?
3. What does the interviewer ask Citizen 2?
4. What does Citizen 2 think about the location?
5. What do both citizens think about the parking rules?
