Reading

Read the story and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Ben. I am a student. I wake up in the morning and I am happy. I eat food and I drink water. Then, I go to school. I do not go to work. I am a young boy. I go to school and I study. I study English and I read books. I like my school. It is a big school.

My father and my mother are tall. They do not go to school. They go to work. My father goes to work in a car. The car is fast. My mother goes to work. She works in a big building. They work in the morning and in the afternoon.

After school, I do not study. I go to the park. The park is a good place. I play in the park. I play with my big dog. The dog runs fast. I play with my sister too. My sister is small and she is fast. We are happy in the park.

At night, I go home. I go to my house. I eat hot food with my family. I drink cold water. I am tired. I do not play at night. I sleep in my big bed. I sleep and I am ready for the morning. I like my day. I like to study and I like to play. What do you do in your day? Where do you go?

Questions:

1. Where does Ben go in the morning?
2. What does Ben do at school?
3. Where do his father and mother go?
4. Where does Ben go after school?
5. Does Ben play at night?
