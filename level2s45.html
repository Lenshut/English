Speaking Tasks

Practice executing the 'Speed Dating' interaction aloud without stopping.

Task A: Full Roleplay (Speed Dating)
Partner:
"Hi, my name is Alex. I like to travel. What about you?"
You:
"Hi Alex. I am [Name]. I like to travel too! Where did you go last year?"
Partner:
"I went to New York. I think it is the best city."
You:
"Really? I disagree. I prefer London to New York because it is older."
Partner:
"That is interesting. Well, do you want to get coffee tomorrow?"
You:
"I am busy tomorrow, but let's go on Friday instead!"

Reading

Read the transcript of a multi-topic interview and answer comprehension questions.

Interview Transcript: Meeting Sarah

Mark: Hi Sarah, it is nice to meet you. Tell me, what do you usually do on the weekend?
Sarah: Nice to meet you, Mark. I usually play tennis or read a book. What about you?
Mark: I usually watch TV. I don't like sports because they are tiring.
Sarah: Really? I think sports are the most important thing for your health.
Mark: I disagree with you. I think eating good food is more important than sports. What do you think about fast food?
Sarah: I never eat fast food because it is unhealthy. I prefer cooking at home.
Mark: Wow, you are very healthy! Are you free tomorrow? Let's go to a healthy restaurant.
Sarah: I would love to, but I am busy tomorrow. Let's go on Saturday instead!

Questions:

1. How does Sarah return the first question to Mark?
2. Why does Mark not like sports?
3. How does Mark disagree with Sarah about sports?
4. How often does Sarah eat fast food?
5. How does Sarah modify Mark's invitation?
