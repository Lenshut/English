Speaking Tasks

Task B: Read Aloud

Task C: Read Aloud

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (Contrasting Routines)
Teacher:
"What time do you wake up?"
You:
"I wake up at 7 o'clock."
Teacher:
"What time does your sister wake up?"
You:
"She wakes up at 8 o'clock."
Teacher:
"Where do you go?"
You:
"I go to school. My father goes to work."
7

Reading

Read the story comparing Tom's and Emma's routines and answer the questions.

Hello! My name is Tom. I am a student. Today, I want to tell you about my routine. My sister, Emma, has a different routine. I wake up very early. I wake up at 6 o'clock. I drink cold water and I eat food. Emma does not wake up at 6 o'clock. She wakes up at 8 o'clock. She sleeps a lot. She drinks milk in the morning.

At 8 o'clock, I go to school. I walk to the school. The school is big. Emma does not go to school. She is a baby. She stays in the house. She plays with her toys in the room.

At 12 o'clock, I eat lunch at school. I eat an apple and bread. Emma eats food at home. She eats soft food. My mother helps Emma.

In the afternoon, I go home at 3 o'clock. Emma and I play in the house. We play with our dog. The dog is fast. It runs in the house.

At night, we are very tired. I sleep at 9 o'clock. I sleep in my bed. Emma sleeps at 7 o'clock. She sleeps early. She sleeps in a small bed. We have different routines, but we are happy.

Questions:

1. What time does Tom wake up?
2. What time does Emma wake up?
3. Does Emma go to school?
4. What time does Tom sleep?
5. What time does Emma sleep?
