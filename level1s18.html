Short Answer

Convert the sentences to the requested form (+, -, ?).

1. Change to Negative (-): I am happy.

2. Change to Question (?): He is a boy.

3. Change to Positive (+): Are you tired?

4. Change to Negative (-): They are students.

5. Change to Question (?): We are teachers.

6. Change to Question (?): She is Mary.

7. Answer Yes: Are they angry?

8. Answer No: Is he a teacher?

9. Write the plural of "I am".

10. Write the plural of "He is not".
