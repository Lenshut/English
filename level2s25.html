6

Speaking Tasks

Practice stating and softening your opinions aloud.

Task A: Read Aloud

"I think learning English is fun, but I do not think it is easy."

Task B: Read Aloud

"I do not think the new movie is good. I think it is very boring."

Task C: Read Aloud

"We think the restaurant is expensive, so we do not think we will go there."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (Debate Prep)
Teacher:
"Let's talk about food. What do you think about pizza?"
You:
"I think pizza is very delicious, but I don't think it is healthy."
Teacher:
"I agree. What do you think about vegetables?"
You:
"I think vegetables are good for you, and I think they are tasty too."
7

Reading

Read the movie review below and extract the subjective opinions of the author.

Movie Review: 'The Dark Forest'

Yesterday, I watched the new movie 'The Dark Forest'. It is a horror movie. I think the actors are very good, but I do not think the story is interesting. It is very slow.

My friend loved it, but I think it is boring. Also, I do not think the music is scary at all. It sounds like happy pop music!

Overall, I think you should save your money. I do not believe this movie is worth watching at the cinema. I think it is better to watch it at home.

Questions:

1. What does the writer think about the actors?
2. What does the writer think about the story?
3. Does the writer think the movie is exciting or boring?
4. What does the writer think about the music?
5. Where does the writer think you should watch the movie?
