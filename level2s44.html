Speaking Tasks

Practice delivering your persuasive sales pitch aloud.

Task A: Read Aloud

"I think you should buy this phone because it is faster and more beautiful than your old phone."

Task B: Read Aloud

"I think winter is the best season because the snow is beautiful, and it is better than the hot summer."

Task C: Read Aloud

"I disagree with you. I prefer dogs to cats because dogs are more friendly, and they are the best pets."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay (The Debate)
Teacher:
"I think reading books is boring. Watching TV is the best."
You:
"I completely disagree with you. I think reading is better than TV."
Teacher:
"Really? Why do you think that?"
You:
"Because books make you think, and they are more interesting than TV shows."
7

Reading

Read the persuasive sales brochure and analyze the opinions and reasoning.

The Super Blender 3000!

Are you looking for a new blender for your kitchen? We think you should buy the Super Blender 3000. Why? Because it is the best blender in the world!

First, we think it is better than older blenders because it is much faster. You can make a smoothie in ten seconds! It is also quieter than other machines. You will love it because it does not make a loud noise in the morning.

Some people think it is too expensive. We disagree! We think it is a great price because it will last for ten years. It is the most beautiful and the most powerful machine for your home. Buy it today!

Questions:

1. Why does the brochure say you should buy the Super Blender?
2. Why is it better than older blenders?
3. Why will you love it in the morning?
4. What do some people think about the price?
5. Why does the company disagree about the price?
