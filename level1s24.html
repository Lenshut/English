7

Reading

Read the story and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Ben. I am a student. I am a tall boy. Look at my family. Who is the tall man? He is my father. He is very fast. Who is the short woman? She is my mother. She is very nice. Who is the small girl? She is my sister. She is short and small. We are a happy family.

Where are we today? We are in the park. The park is a big place. It is a very hot day. The sun is big and hot in the sky. I am very hot. I want cold water. I drink the cold water. My sister is hot too. She drinks cold water.

Look at the tall tree! What is that? That is a bird. The bird is small. It is in the tree. Where is my dog? My dog is not in the tree. My dog is under the tree. He is a big, black dog. The dog is fast. He plays with my father.

What is this? This is my bag. The bag is on the grass. What is in the bag? My book is in the bag. It is a big, blue book. I read the book in the park. My sister has a red ball. The ball is under the chair.

Look out! What is that? That is a car. The car is on the street. It is a very fast car. It is not slow. I like the big park. I like the tall trees and the small birds. I do not like the hot sun, but the cold water is good. Where are you today? Are you in a park or in a house? What is near you?

Questions:

1. Who is the short woman?
2. Where is Ben's family today?
3. Is it a hot day or a cold day?
4. Where is the big dog?
5. What is in Ben's bag?
