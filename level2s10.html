Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"You look sad. Are you always sad?"
You:
"No, I am never sad! I am usually happy."
Teacher:
"Are you busy today?"
You:
"Yes, I am always busy on Mondays. I always work hard."

Reading

Read the character profiles describing states of being with adverbs of frequency.

Meet The Twins: Leo and Max

Leo and Max are brothers, but they are very different. Leo is always energetic. He is never tired. He always wakes up at 5:00 AM and he usually runs in the park. He is always happy and he smiles all the time.

Max, on the other hand, is usually sleepy. He is sometimes late for work because he never wakes up early. He is usually hungry, so he always eats big meals. He is never energetic in the morning.

They are always brothers, but their habits are never the same!

Questions:

1. Is Leo ever tired?
2. What is Leo always?
3. Why is Max sometimes late?
4. Is Max ever energetic in the morning?
5. Are their habits ever the same?
