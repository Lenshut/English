Reading

Read the short story and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Sarah. I am in my house today. Look at my room. It is a big room. Where is my book? The book is not on the bed. It is on the desk. The red pen is next to the book on the desk.

Where is my cat? The cat is not in the box. The cat is under the bed. It is a small, white cat. The cat is very fast. Where is my dog? The dog is not under the bed. The dog is in the box! It is a big, black dog. The dog is very happy in the big box.

Where is my mother? She is not in my room. She is in the kitchen. She is a tall woman. She is very nice. Where is my brother? He is in the garden. He is a short boy. He is very fast.

Look at the ball. Where is the ball? The ball is under the chair. The chair is next to the desk. I like my room. It is a very good room. The dog is in the box, the cat is under the bed, and the book is on the desk. Where are you? Are you in a house? Are you in a school? I am in my big room, and I am very happy.
