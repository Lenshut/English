Speaking Tasks

Practice running through the speaking test format aloud.

Task A: Part 1 - Introduction

Teacher: "Tell me about your daily routine."

"I usually wake up at... and I always... but I never..."

Task B: Part 2 - The Past

Teacher: "What did you do last weekend?"

"Last weekend I went to... and I ate... but I did not..."

Task C: Part 3 - The Future

Teacher: "What are you going to do tomorrow?"

"Tomorrow, I am going to... and I will..."

Task D: Part 4 - Opinion

Teacher: "Which is better, the city or the country?"

"I think the city is better because... What about you?"

Reading

Read the mock exam instructions and sample reading passage.

Instructions for the Final Written Exam

Welcome to the Level 2 Final Exam. You have 45 minutes to complete this test. Do not talk to other students. Read every question carefully.

Sample Passage:
Last year, Mark went to Japan. He bought the ticket because he always wanted to see Tokyo. Tokyo is bigger than his city, and he thinks it is the most exciting place in the world. Next year, he is going to travel to London. He hopes it will be as good as Japan.

Questions (Based on Sample Passage):

1. Where did Mark go last year?
2. Why did he buy the ticket?
3. Is Tokyo bigger or smaller than his city?
4. What does he think is the most exciting place?
5. Where is he going to travel next year?
