Reading • Level 1

Foundation Reading

Build your core reading skills. Practice reading simple sentences, identifying basic facts, matching vocabulary, and understanding short stories about daily life.

📖 Read & Answer

"Hello! My name is Anna. I am a student. I am 10 years old. I live in a big house. I have a red bag. My teacher is Mr. Smith. He is tall and happy. I like school because I have many friends."

✅ True or False

"David is a doctor. He works in a hospital. He wakes up at 6 o'clock every morning. He drinks black coffee. He does not like tea. He drives a blue car to work. He helps sick people."
1. David is a teacher.
2. He wakes up at 6:00 AM.
3. David likes tea very much.
4. He drives a blue car.
5. David helps sick people.

🔍 Vocabulary in Context

Read the sentence and guess the meaning of the underlined word.

1. The sky is blue.

2. I sit on a chair in the classroom.

3. She is my sister. We have the same mother.

4. I am very hungry. I want to eat pizza.

5. The dog is under the table.

📑 Sequencing

Read the sentences. Put them in the correct order (1, 2, 3, 4) to make a story.

A. I go to school at 8 o'clock.
B. I eat breakfast with my family.
C. I wake up at 7 o'clock in the morning.
D. I learn English in the classroom.

💡 Main Idea

"Cats are small animals. They have four legs and a long tail. Cats like to sleep in the sun. They like to drink milk and eat fish. Many people have cats in their house. They are good pets."
