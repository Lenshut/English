LEVEL 5 : UPPER-INTERMEDIATE

Program Overview

Welcome to Level 5. This program transforms students into advanced, natural communicators. Students will master perfect tense continuous forms, sophisticated debate tactics, formal vs. informal register switching, and critical problem-solving in high-stakes environments.

Skill Progression Path

Skill expansion at Start (Ph 1), Midpoint (Ph 4), and Goal (Ph 8).

🏆 Master Goal

Speak fluidly in 8–12 sentence blocks. Defend complex opinions using advanced connectors, recount layered narratives utilizing past perfect tenses, seamlessly adapt language register to the audience, and navigate unpredictable, spontaneous dialogues with native-like agility.
