Writing • Level 5

Upper-Intermediate Writing

Refine your professional tone. Practice Perfect Continuous tenses, Past Perfect narratives, formal register shifts, and logical debate outlines.

💡 10 Writing Topics

Choose a topic below and write a detailed, complex paragraph (8-10 sentences) using Level 5 structures.

1. Ongoing Effort: Describe a project you are working on using Present Perfect Continuous (I have been...).
2. A Big Realization: Use Past Perfect to explain a mistake you discovered (I realized I had...).
3. Emphasize a Point: Write a complaint focusing emotion with a Cleft sentence (What bothers me is...).
4. Direct Contrast: Compare two completely different things using 'Whereas' or 'While'.
5. Compressed Narrative: Write a story beginning with a Participle Clause (Exhausted from the heat,...).
6. Diplomatic Bad News: Write an email delivering bad news to a client using softeners (slight complication).
7. Professional Intro: Draft a formal cover letter introduction using PPS and PPC to list your experience.
8. Hypothetical Brainstorm: Propose a wild idea for your company starting with 'Suppose we...'.
9. Tag Question Dialogue: Write a dialogue where one person manipulates the other using tag questions.
10. Root Cause Analysis: Explain a failure using 'The underlying issue is not X, but rather Y'.

🗣️ Debate Outline

Prepare a structured outline for a debate. Topic: "Social media does more harm than good."

Arguments FOR the motion (It is harmful)

Write 3 distinct points. Use sequence words (Primarily, Subsequently, Ultimately).

Arguments AGAINST the motion (It is beneficial)

Write 3 distinct points. Use logical fallacies to attack the first side (Just because... doesn't necessarily mean).
