7

Reading

Read the short story and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Anna. I am a student. I am not tall. I am a short girl. Look at my family! My father is a very tall man. He is not short. My mother is short.

Look at our house! The house is very big. It is a big, red house. It is not small. In the house, I have a dog and a cat. The dog is very big. The dog is black. The cat is very small. The cat is white.

Today, I go to the zoo with my family. The zoo is a big place. Look at the animals! The elephant is very, very big. It is a big animal. The mouse is very small. It is a small animal. The elephant is next to the mouse.

Look at the giraffe! The giraffe is a tall animal. It is very tall. It is not short. Look at the monkey. The monkey is a small animal. I like the zoo. I like the tall giraffe and the big elephant. I do not like the small mouse. The zoo is fun!

Questions:

1. Is Anna a tall girl or a short girl?
2. Is the house big or small?
3. What color is the small cat?
4. Is the giraffe a short animal?
5. What animals does Anna like?
