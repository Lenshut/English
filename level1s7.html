7

Reading

Read the passage below and answer the questions.

Hello, my name is Lisa. I am a student.

This is my classroom.

Look! What is this?

It is a desk.

What is this?

It is a chair. I sit on the chair.

What is this?

It is a book. I have two books.

Look! What is this?

It is a pen.

And what is this?

It is a pencil. I write with a pencil.

My classroom is nice.

Questions:

1. Who is Lisa? (She is a...)
2. What does Lisa sit on? (It is a...)
3. How many books does Lisa have?
4. What does Lisa write with?
5. Is it a pen or a pencil?
