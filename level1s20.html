Reading

Read the short story and answer the questions below.

Hello. I am David. Today is a very hot day. The sun is big and hot. I do not like hot days. I am very hot. I want cold water. I drink cold water and I eat cold ice cream. I am happy.

Look at the street! I see a red car. The car is very fast. It is not slow. Look at the park. I see a small turtle. The turtle is very slow. It is not fast. I like the slow turtle.

I sit under a tall tree. The tree is big. I am not hot now. The water is cold and good.

Questions:

1. Is the day hot or cold?
2. What does David want?
3. Is the red car fast or slow?
4. Is the turtle fast?
5. Is David hot under the tree?
