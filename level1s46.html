7

Reading

Read the story about Lisa surviving in a new town and answer the questions.

Hello! My name is Lisa. I am a tourist in a big town today. The town is very nice. I want to buy a new shirt and new shoes. I do not know where the clothes store is. I see a nice woman. I say, "Excuse me, please. Where is the clothes store?"

The nice woman smiles. She says, "Go straight. Turn left at the big hospital. The store is next to the bank." I say, "Thank you very much!" She says, "You're welcome."

I go straight and turn left at the hospital. I see the clothes store! It is next to the bank. I go into the store. I see a blue shirt. I like the blue shirt. I say to the man, "Excuse me, how much is this shirt?" The man says, "It is ten dollars." I say, "I want to buy the shirt, please."

Then, I see black shoes. Remember, shoes are plural! I ask the man, "How much are these black shoes?" The man says, "The shoes are twenty dollars." I say, "Thank you, but I do not want to buy the shoes. I only want the shirt."

I give the man ten dollars. The man says, "Thank you! Have a good day." I say, "You're welcome." I walk out of the store. I am very happy. I have a new shirt, and I can ask for directions and prices! Survival English is very good.

Questions:

1. Where does Lisa want to go?
2. Where is the store? (next to...)
3. How much is the blue shirt?
4. How much are the black shoes?
5. Does Lisa buy the shoes?
