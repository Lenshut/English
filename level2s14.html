6

Speaking Tasks

Practice pronouncing the three different '-ed' sounds (/t/, /d/, /id/) aloud.

Task A: /t/ sound

"I worked hard and I watched a movie."

Task B: /d/ sound

"I played tennis and I cleaned my room."

Task C: /id/ sound

"I wanted to go, so I waited for the bus."

Task D: Teacher Roleplay
Teacher:
"What did you do last night?"
You:
"I cooked dinner and I watched TV. Then, I cleaned the kitchen."
Teacher:
"Did you play any games?"
You:
"Yes! I played a game with my brother and we talked for an hour."
7

Reading

Read a diary entry about 'yesterday' and list the regular past actions.

Yesterday was a busy day. I woke up and I cleaned my apartment. Then, I walked to the supermarket. I shopped for food and I carried the bags home.

In the afternoon, I cooked a big dinner. I invited my friends. They arrived at 7 PM. We talked, we laughed, and we listened to music.

After they left, I washed the dishes and I watched a short movie. I rested because I was very tired.

Questions:

1. What did the writer clean?
2. How did they go to the supermarket?
3. What did they do in the afternoon?
4. What time did the friends arrive?
5. What did they do after the friends left?
