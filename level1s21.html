7

Reading

Read the short story and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Tom. I am a student. I am ten years old. I am a happy boy. Look at this picture! It is a picture of my family. I have a big family. They are very good.

Who is the tall man? He is my father. My father is very tall and big. He is a happy man. Who is the short woman? She is my mother. My mother is short and small. She is very nice. They are my mother and my father.

Look at the tall boy! Who is he? He is my brother. My brother is fifteen years old. He is very fast. He is a tall boy. He is not short.

Look at the small girl! Who is she? She is my sister. My sister is five years old. She is very small and very short. She is a happy girl. She is not sad.

The dog is in the picture too. Who is the dog? It is my good dog. The dog is black and white. It is a big dog. The dog is very fast. It is not slow. My family and my dog are very happy. It is a hot day in the picture, but we are not sad. We are happy.

Who are they? They are my family. My family is big. The picture is very nice. I love my family. My father is tall, my mother is short, my brother is fast, and my sister is small. We are a very happy family today.
