LEVEL 2 : ELEMENTARY

Program Overview

Welcome to the Level 2 Tagasa English Program. This 6-month program bridges the gap between basic survival phrases and true conversational ability. Students will master past/future tenses, express detailed opinions, and engage in multi-turn dialogues.

Skill Progression Path

Skill expansion at Start (Ph 1), Midpoint (Ph 4), and Goal (Ph 8).

🏆 Master Goal

Transition from isolated single sentences to fluid paragraphs. Speak confidently about past memories and future plans, articulate clear opinions with reasons, and independently sustain a natural, back-and-forth conversation.
