7

Reading

Read the story about Tom's busy day and answer the questions below.

Hello! My name is Tom. Today is a very hot day. The sun is big and hot in the sky. I am in the park with my friends, Mark and Lily. We play football in the park. We run very fast.

After we play, we are very hot. I do not want to play. I want to rest. I am hot, so I want cold water. I say to Mark, "Do you want water?" Mark says, "Yes, I do. I want cold water too." Lily says, "I do not want water. I want cold apple juice." We drink cold water and sweet apple juice under a big tree.

Then, we walk home. I go to my house. I am very happy, but I am tired. I look at my mother. I say, "Mother, I want food." My mother says, "Do you want fish?" I say, "No, I do not want fish. I want meat and rice, please." My mother cooks hot meat and white rice. I eat the good food.

After I eat, it is night. I look at my bed. I say, "I do not want to play. I want to sleep." I sleep in my big bed. I sleep very well. I like the park, but I want to rest at night. What do you want today? Do you want to play or do you want to sleep?

Questions:

1. What does Tom want in the park?
2. What does Lily want in the park?
3. Does Tom want fish for dinner?
4. What does Tom want to eat?
5. What does Tom want to do at night?
