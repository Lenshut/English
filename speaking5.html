Speaking • Level 5

Upper-Intermediate Speaking

Master the nuances of narrative and negotiation. Practice complex storytelling with Past Perfect, deliver de-escalation strategies, and sustain unscripted professional small talk.

📖 The Past in the Past (Past Perfect)

Relate two past events correctly. Use: 'By the time I..., I had already...'

Task: The Missing Flight

Explain a situation where you were late for a flight or meeting.
(e.g. "By the time I reached the gate, the plane had already departed because I had forgotten my passport.")

🧘 Conflict Resolution

A customer is furious. Validating their feelings before pivoting to a solution.

Prompt: An Unacceptable Delay

Roleplay de-escalating a client whose project is 2 weeks late.

Key phrases: "I understand why you are frustrated...", "Let me look into this immediately...", "We are willing to offer a discount..."

🤝 Professional Small Talk

Keep the conversation flowing at a corporate event.

Opening: "How are you finding the conference so far?"
Follow-up: "How long have you been working in this sector?"
Pivoting: "Speaking of technology, have you seen the new AI platform?"

💡 Collaborative Thinking

Suggesting and evaluating multiple paths forward.

Structure: "Why don't we...?"

Suggest a solution for decreasing sales in your imaginary company.

Structure: "Alternatively..."

Provide a second, backup plan if the first one is too expensive.

📈 Data-Driven Pitch

Present a trend using 'As you can see' and emphasis adverbs.

Task: Describe a Growth Chart

Describe a line going up sharply over 3 years.

  • "As you can see on this graph..."
  • "Sales have increased significantly since 2022."
  • "The primary reason for this trajectory is..."
